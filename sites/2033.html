Hallelujah!
Viewers all over the world - thank you.
Let's take our reading from the Book of 1 John 4:1:
"Beloved, do not believe every spirit,
but test the spirits whether they are of God;
because many false prophets have gone out into the world.
By this you know the Spirit of God:
Every spirit that confesses that Jesus Christ has come in the flesh is of God,
and every spirit that does not confess that Jesus Christ has come in the flesh is not of God.
And this is the spirit of the Antichrist, which you have heard was coming, and is now already in the world.
You are of God, little children, and have overcome them,
because He who is in you is greater than he who is in the world."
This will lead us to the Book of Romans 3:10 and the Book of Isaiah 38:4,
and also the Book of Luke 7:2.
We have heard the message.
Beloved, not every spirit but prove the spirits, whether they are of God,
because many false prophets are gone out into the world.
The question: how do we tell who is a true prophet and a false prophet?
That is the question now.
We can take our title:
A TRUE PROPHET
The one having the divine Spirit that comes from above
is meek, peaceable, humble and refrains from all impurities of this world.
The Bible says, "He contents himself with fewer wants than those other men."
This Spirit speaks only when God wishes it to speak.
Tell your neighbour, "This Spirit speaks only when God wishes it to speak."
People today, think that healing, blessing, breakthrough
and all of God's blessings are wrought at will by the person concerned.
This is why, today when you see a prophet, you ask him for prayer
not minding whether it is the right time or not.
When you see a prophet - "Pray for me."
A prophet will not pray without Spirit intimation, suggestion,
"Pray! T.B. Joshua, pray, in Jesus' name."
Without Spirit's suggestion T.B. Joshua will not pray.
That is why there is breakthrough.
Others are always ready to pray.
When you say, "Pastor, prophet, pray for me" - "In the name of Jesus, in the blood of Jesus..."
In each instance, a prophet will not open his mouth to pray without Spirit intimation.
This is why when you meet a true prophet of God and say "Pray for me", he will just say, "No, you are blessed. Go."
A true prophet shall prophecy that which he shall live to see accomplished
because intimation is given by the Spirit.
Let me take you to the Book of John 7:6.
"Then Jesus said to them, 'My time has not yet come, but your time is always ready' ".
When He was called to go out and perform a miracle, He said, "No. It is not the time.
Until I have heard from the Father, I will not do so."
Take it from verse 4.
"For no one does anything in secret while he himself seeks to be known openly.
If you do these things, show yourself to the world.
For even His brothers did not believe in Him.
Then Jesus said to them, 'My time has not yet come, but your time is always ready' ".
That means others are always ready to pray.
They say, "Pray for me" - they pray.
No. Not at our will but the will of the Father.
Let me give you a short story: A tribute to my late mother.
She advised me to look into my love walk.
When I do not seem to receive from God, I should look into my love walk.
If I say, "God, give me this biscuit" and after the prayer for many days - no.
She said to me, "Look into your love walk."
Love your neighbour as yourself - look into that, work on that.
When I was in primary school, each time I came back from school,
I would learn that my mummy was in the mountain praying.
I would not bother to go home but would go straight to the mountain.
My mummy would be there praying.
I too became a prayer warrior from a little age.
Sometimes, she would give me an assignment.
She would say, "I used to have a nightmare in so and so area" and that I should fast.
By the time I prayed and fasted, she would call me and say that my prayer had been answered.
That made me to know then that prayer has power.
She would tell me, "The prayer you offered for me - I stopped having that nightmare."
That gladdened my heart that this is the way to talk to God!
This is the road to talk to God; this is the roadmap.
Yes, that is my mum.
