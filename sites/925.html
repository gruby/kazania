GOD IS STILL SAYING SOMETHING
On the 24th of September 2017, during a live Sunday service,
Prophet T.B. Joshua prophesied concerning disastrous storms ganging up in the sea,
adding that a part of Africa would also be affected.
These were his words.
There is disaster - bad nature, storms from the sea.
I said they are ganging up in the sea.
I said this will move from one country to another.
I said it.
It is not yet over because even in Africa,
a part of Africa - I am seeing this will move there.
TT: Mel Flowers 29/06/2018
RV ROBERT BEGIER 12/08/2018
