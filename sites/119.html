TESTIMONY CONTINUES
My name is Mariana Parkhotyuk and I am from Washington State, USA.
It started when I came to The SCOAN last year for Prophet T.B. Joshua's 49th birthday.
He told the congregation, 'This is the day you will remember,
the day of your breakthrough in you career, finances and all issues in your life.'
I took that for myself.
I also had the privilege to meet Prophet T.B. Joshua and I received the Anointing Water.
I went back to the USA and after a few months, a job position opened.
As you can see here, this was the job that was open.
The job description states, 'Clinical Application Specialist'.
I was working as a front office assistant before I applied for this job position.
I have underlined the qualifications, education
and also the experience needed for this job position.
Church of God, this job position required me to have four years of experience
as well as a degree;
I did not have anything within these job description qualifications.

In the first two interviews, they rebuked me

Before the third and last interview, I took this piece of paper and the Anointing Water

Praise the Lord! Put your hands together for Jesus Christ!
After the last interview - the third one, they called me a few weeks later
and told me that I had received the job position.
Not only did I receive the job position, I got a double increase in my salary.
Hallelujah!
After some time, they sent me to a certain place out of State
so that I could receive certification so that I can work in my job.
These are the two certificates.
They sent me and paid for my accommodation and education
and I received these certificates enabling me to work in this job.
Glory be to God. Let us put our hands together for Jesus Christ!
I want to let all the viewers around the world and everyone sitting here know

I would always go but never finish because I was always limited with finances.
With this job position, not only did I get an increase in pay
but they are also helping me to go back to school.
Praise the Lord!
Our sister is saying that not only did she receive that promotion and a higher position,
it came along with a higher salary, education and training.
Put your hands together for Jesus Christ!
After this wonderful breakthrough that you have received through the medium of the Anointing Water,

Church of God and all the viewers around the world, God can do the impossible
