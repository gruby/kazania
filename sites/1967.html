TESTIMONY TIME
Praise the Lord!
Children of God, praise the Lord!
Emmanuel!
(God With Us - Matthew 1:23)
Indeed the Lord is with us.
My name is Mr Abueze Ejike Timothy.
I am from Enugu state but we live in Kano.
My testimony is that the God of Prophet T.B. Joshua delivered me
from the spirit of bedwetting for 36 years.
You mean that you were having the problem of bedwetting for 36 years?
Yes. I grew up with it.
It was only when I was 36 years old that I prayed with Emmanuel TV and saw myself out of this mess.
It was a very challenging and difficult time.
The issue of bedwetting is more serious than poverty
because with poverty, man or a friend can help you but with bedwetting, nobody can help you.
I was unable to help myself or find a solution to that problem.
I tried, I prayed, I fasted.
Brethren, to be sincere, there was a time that I fasted from 6am to 8pm every day
for more than four months because of that problem but nothing came out of it.
I fasted again - four days dry fasting.
The day I completed the four days fasting, I bedwet.
I was helpless and nothing could make me happy when I was having that problem.
It was so severe that I knew that only God could help me.
I tried my best but nothing came out of it until a friend introduced me to Emmanuel TV.
What was your wife's reaction?
To be frank with you, my wife did not know I was having that problem.
She found out one night when she woke up and saw the bed wet.
It used to happen to me in my dreams.
I would be in the midst of people in my dream and excuse myself to go to the restroom.
Then, I would go to the toilet in my dream.
Immediately after going to the restroom, it would happen in the physical.
That night, my wife found out that the bed was wet.
You mean that before you married your wife, you never told her that you had the problem of bedwetting?
No, she was not aware of it.
You knew you were going to be always with your wife,
so why did you not tell her about that problem you were having?
I would like to use this medium to thank her because she stood by me, even after noticing it.
She was the one who used to wash the bedsheets and encourage me that one day, it would be a testimony.
Today, by God's grace, it is now a testimony.
Hallelujah!
At that time, when something good was about to happen in my life,
when a breakthrough was about to happen in my life, I would pray.
As I continued to pray, the next thing that would happen is that I would bedwet.
After I would bedwet, that thing would not come to pass; it would be a failure.
That was what I had been experiencing all these years until I got my deliverance through Emmanuel TV.
Brethren, I did not enjoy anything like social gatherings, especially if it involved sleeping elsewhere.
I just didn't do it.
I would make sure I had an excuse to either leave before night or I would not go at all.
That was how it had been.
The enemy suppressed me so much because before I got my deliverance,
I would be in a dream and would feel pressed down.
I would be unable to move myself.
It was as if I was about to die.
Sometimes, a masquerade with strange faces would come to me.
It would hook and paralyse me.
I would be aware but not able to raise my arms or legs.
I was unable to wake up; it would come like a seizure.
It would grip me to the bed.
It was only after the torment when I was released that I could move myself.
That was how it had been all these years and it was very difficult for me to come out of it.
A friend of mine who had been watching Emmanuel TV for a long time, introduced me to it.
I bought the cable, installed it and began to see what was happening.
I began to see what God was using this ministry to do in the lives of people.
Hope came to me that I would be free one day.
During the mass prayer, I would gather my family and we would pray together
and most especially, during the time of prayer with Prophet T.B. Joshua -
'Distance is not a barrier' - I would lay my two hands upon the screen
and begin to pray along with the man of God.
I would pray that everything that was bringing shame to my life,
in the name of the God of Prophet T.B. Joshua, out of my life!
Brethren, before I knew it, I began to see the man of God in my dreams.
Now, to the glory of God, I might urinate in the dream but will not wet my bed.
Praise the Lord!
Put your hands together for Jesus!
Hallelujah!
You mean that after praying with the man of God, Prophet T.B. Joshua, on Emmanuel TV,
that problem of bedwetting for 36 years came to an end?
Yes, not only the problem of bedwetting, even the pressing
and the things I used to see in my dreams sometimes like snakes and other things,
I no longer experience them and I am free.
I am totally free, in Jesus' name! Thank You, Jesus!
So now you are no longer experiencing that problem of bedwetting?
It is now a thing of joy and a testimony.
I am now sharing it to the Glory of God.
The enemy has been kicked out of my life completely, in Jesus' name.
- Hallelujah!
- Praise the Lord!
My advice to everyone hearing or watching me
is that Emmanuel TV is not just an ordinary station that we watch like movies.
It is a must for every home if you are really a believer because God is doing much through this medium.
My advice is to follow the Word of God in John 14:6, that Jesus Christ in the Way, the Truth and the Life.
Whatsoever one is passing through in life, Jesus is the only Way.
He is not one of the ways; I have tried Him and found that He is the only Way.
He will remain the only answer to every issue of my life and yours, in the name of Jesus.
Hallelujah. Put your hands together once again for Jesus!
TT Gary Tonge 18/08/2020
