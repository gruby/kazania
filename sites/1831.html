God Is Still Saying Something
On the 29th of December 2013, Prophet T.B. Joshua prophesied a coming challenge in the aviation industry
that would negatively affect both passengers and airlines.
Let's listen to him.
Those of us who are into the business of
airlines, aeroplanes, flying or whatever,
you will be at the airport for two or three days and you may not get a flight to go to where you are going
because the demand will be more than supply.
There will be a shortage of flying -
airlines, airplane shortage.
It will be so difficult.
The business will not be as usual.
Most especially in Africa.
It will affect Africa so much -
the African Continent, most especially.
If your business is about flying from one country to another,
I think there will be a challenge.
TT Gary Tonge 17/05/2020
