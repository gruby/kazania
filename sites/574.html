My name is Chioma, I came from Anambra State (Nigeria).
The problem that brought me to The SCOAN was
that there was a demon that used to appear to me physically.
She said she was my sister.
Does it mean that demon was a woman?
Yes, she looked like me.
It started at my tender age, she followed me around everywhere I went.
Anything I did, she did it with me.
It appeared to you physically?
Yes, she appeared to me physically.
And she always followed you?
She always followed me anywhere I went and in anything I did.
People around me would not see her.
Anything I put on, she would put on the same.
We looked alike, the same size, everything.
She looked just like me.
In everything?
Yes.
Even when they put food on the table, she would eat with me
and people around me would say that I was eating too much.
But they would not know that I was eating with her.
When I was in school, in my National Diploma 1, she came to me
and said that she was not in school, so I should drop out.
That demon that followed her said, "You know, I am not in school and you are in school.
Whatever I do, it's what you should do. So, leave the school".
She said I should drop out; I asked her why.
She said she was my twin sister and I should ask my mum about her.
I called my mum and asked her about the spirit.
You mean you asked your mummy physically?
Yes, I asked my mum physically.
My mum said she didn't know what I was saying.
I said okay; after my IT, I wouldn't go back
because she said I should drop out so I dropped out.
She told me her name was Chi-Chi and I called her Chi-Chi.
- And you called her Chi-Chi?
- I called her Chi-Chi.
People around you used to hear you when you called Chi-Chi?
No, unless it was in the night.
That does not mean, if you bear the name Chi-Chi, you should remove that name.
Please take note of that.
People around you, they did hear you when you called that name?
No, it was only in the night she would come to me
and start talking with me.
People around me would be hearing a voice.
My mum would ask who I was talking to but I would say nobody; she would go back.
But in the daytime, we talked with the mind.
- You talked with what?
- Mind.
When I would walk on the road, if I had a problem with anybody,
she would come to me and ask, "What do you want me to do with the person?"
I would say I wanted to beat the person; she would then join me to beat them.
So, what would happen to the person?
Give us account of one or two persons you people have beaten before and what happened.
There are many. Even in my school and at home.
Sometimes even my dad would be wondering what was going on and ask,
"Are you a lion or what?"
I would say, "No, I am not a lion. It's my strength".
Not knowing there were two people.
Yes, not knowing that we were two.
My friend in school - we beat her.
At home, my brother too - we beat him very well.
Even my elder ones, I beat them too.
Was there a time your mummy misbehaved, and the evil spirit beat her?
Your mummy or your dad?
There was a time when I was beating my brother that my dad came
and started flogging me; I told him if not that he was my dad,
I would have beaten him and he would have been on the ground by now.
Did you listen to that?
Where is your father?
He is at home.
So, after that, when I got married, she came to me and said,
"You are married, I am not married, do you think that the marriage will hold?"
I asked why. She said that she didn't like my husband
and as she was not married, I should leave the marriage.
I said okay. In time, I had a problem with my husband.
I would talk to her and she would advise me.
She would tell me that she would call him. When my husband came back,
I would ask him, "Did anybody call you named Chi-Chi?"
My husband would say, "I don't know what you are talking about".
When my husband beat me, she would ask me, "What do you want me to do to him?"
She would be saying that it was time for this marriage to end.
At the end, I separated from my husband in February this year.
The spirit would come and appear to me and tell me what would be happening.
I would lie to my people that I had a dream.
Sometimes, she would tell me that I should go out and drink
because she wanted to drink; I would go out, drink, get drunk and come back.
She would get drunk also.
Herself too would get drunk?
Yes.
In the same beer parlour, she would be there with you?
She would be there with me, drinking; if I took two, she would take two.
But the person selling the beer would not see her?
Nobody would see her; it was only me that would see her.
You would be the one who paid for the four (beers)?
Yes, I would be the one to pay.
On Monday, she said that I should not enter the service; I asked why.
She said, "No, let us turn back" but I entered here.
When the wise man was praying, he said, "You demon, out of her" and I fell down.
Since then, I have not been seeing this spirit.
Since that Monday, you have not been seeing your second?
Yes.
How has life been since that Monday?
My life is free; I haven't seen her around me anymore. She doesn't talk.
I don't see anybody like me. I sleep well. No nightmares, no problems.
It had some influence over you.
When you were coming to The SCOAN, why did it not stop you from coming?
She said I should not enter here; I asked, "Why? I want to go".
She said, "No, let's turn back fast"; I turned and said, "I will go".
Each time you went to the market, what happened?
Each time I went to the market, she would be discussing with me, mind to mind.
She would be telling me what I needed, where I should go;
we would be walking together.
Each time you travelled, what happened?
Each time I travelled, I would be afraid because she would tell me
that my time was up; I was afraid that I would have an accident -
she would be putting accidents in my mind.
Have you attempted deliverance before?
My father and my in-law took me somewhere.
You are just a new-born baby now.
Old things passed away; every other thing became new.
