My name is Chukwuka Cletus Onwuesi.
I'm from Anambra State (Nigeria), Oka precisely.
The issues that brought me here are about my education
and things that have been happening in my life
from the day I came across a post on social media.
The title was, 'A fallen angel'.
When I saw the post, I became so interested that
immediately I just set my eyes on the post,
I felt like something was attracting me to look at it more.
And when I was reading it, it was saying things about the way
the angels were chased out from Heaven and are somewhere hanging on the air,
and some were on the rivers and some fallen on the ground.
But from that day, even whilst I was reading it, it was like a movement,
it was like something entered inside of me.
I have not remained the same again from that day.
And from that day, I started misbehaving.
It took me into the world; I started drinking alcohol,
things that I have not done before.
I saw myself drinking alcohol and following bad gangs -
doing things that I had never thought I could do in my life.
Each time I tried to stop doing it, I still found myself doing it again.
Sometimes when I drank, I would stay until midnight; sometimes, I would not go back home.
I would stay at my friend's place 3-4 days before I would go back home.
When I went back home, I would be like, "How did I do this?"
But next time, I would see myself doing it again.
From that time, it damaged my life; it caused a lot of damage to me.
