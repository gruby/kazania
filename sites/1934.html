TESTIMONY CONTINUES
People of God, Emmanuel!
(God With Us - Matthew 1:23)
My name is Mr Waziri Fuwanu from Taraba State.
This is my lovely wife, Blessing Fuwanu Waziri, also from Taraba State.
My testimony is an Emmanuel TV testimony.
As you are seeing here, I am very happy to have this opportunity to testify
to the glory of the Lord in my life.
I was addicted to drinking alcohol, especially beer.
When I say 'addiction', it is not like ordinarily taking one or two bottles.
I could finish 14 bottles in a day.
In my car, you would always see a bottle or can of beer.
For how many years?
For 17 years, I had been drinking.
- For 17 good years?
- Yes.
- You have been under the influence of alcohol?
- Yeah.
Sir, we want you to tell us:
How has this spirit of drunkenness affected you?
Oh my God! It has affected me financially.
As you are seeing me here, I am an accountant serving with the Taraba State Primary Education Board.
I didn't know what I was doing with the money; I couldn't keep it in my account.
My car was my bank; I would always keep my money in my car.
If I went out and saw any beer parlour, I would park and drink.
On Sunday, I wouldn't go to church.
I would park my car at a beer parlour and drink with my friends.
When I entered the beer parlour, they would all hail me, expecting a free drink.
I really struggled with this addiction.
My mum even said she was going to curse me if I didn't stop drinking.
The more I tried to stop it, the more it persisted.
Until one fateful day, I was watching Emmanuel TV during a live Sunday service.
I saw the man of God moving in the midst of the congregation, prophesying to them.
He met one man and asked if he could speak to him.
The man said, "Yes".
He said, "You are drinking beer. If you don't stop drinking, they will poison you."
I was in my parlour with my wife; I just stood up.
Something told me, "See, this is your miracle! Go and touch the television."
Something pushed me; I just walked up and touched the screen of Emmanuel TV.
I said, "I claim this prophecy, in Jesus' name. I will never drink again!"
I forgot myself completely and for two weeks, I didn't drink!
Put your hands together for Jesus!
Yes, He is worthy of our praises.
After that - what happened?
As I touched the screen, I just felt a cold sensation in my heart.
I didn't go out that day at all. In fact, for two weeks I didn't go out to drink.
One day, I said to my wife, "It's been almost two weeks without drinking."
My wife said, "God of T.B. Joshua has done it!"
Let us put our hands together for our Lord, Jesus Christ!
Sir, now that you have been set free, tell us - are you still having that urge?
Now, I don't like beer.
Even if you promise me N1,000,000 to enter a beer parlour,
you can take it away and let me remain in poverty.
Some people asked how I managed to leave this addiction to drinking beer.
I told them that the God of Prophet T.B. Joshua had done it.
I would open my phone and show them the picture of Prophet T.B. Joshua.
They glorified God and said they also planned to come to The SCOAN.
I told them that I didn't even come to The SCOAN but just prayed with Emmanuel TV.
They were shocked.
Some of my friends even acquired Emmanuel TV.
Amen!
Hallelujah!
When I was in this trouble, I would not buy good clothes for myself
but I would mostly buy drinks for other people.
I could not buy good clothes; I didn't even take care of my wife.
If she asked me for N1,000, I would not give her but I could drink from N3,000 to N5,000 outside every day.
To God be the glory. Let us put our hands together for our Lord and Saviour, Jesus Christ.
Yes, with Him all things are possible.
And tell us, sir:
You say you always were buying drinks for your friends,
they were always rejoicing when you were in the beer parlour.
And now, with this drastic change that occurred in your life,
can you tell us what happened to you?
It is really a miracle.
My friends who I was doing this with, I advise them to stop drinking alcohol.
When I received this miracle, from there I began to know the purpose of life.
The purpose of life is to glorify God, not to be going out drinking.
On Sunday, when people are supposed to be in the church,
you will see some of them in a beer parlour.
It is very, very bad!
It affected me spiritually and physically.
I didn't pray in the night.
When I got up in the morning, I would see my wife praying but I couldn't join her.
Somebody that got drunk yesterday would be weak.
Honestly, sometimes I would be hearing my wife praying for God to arrest and change me.
I would tell her to stop disturbing my sleep and let me rest.
But see, the Lord has done it now.
We are living happily!
There is no fighting.
I love her more and more now.
To God be the glory.
Let's us clap for Jesus Christ!
Yes. We thank God Almighty for everything.
Tell us one more thing:
Please, for how long have you stopped drinking?
I stopped drinking alcohol on 28th June 2012.
Up till now, I have not taken it again. I hate it!
You mean no more urge for drinking?
- You are perfectly delivered?
- Yes.
Cleaned, in the name of Jesus, by the blood of Jesus.
Amen!
Put your hands together for Jesus!
Emmanuel TV Testimony
One of my friends is a lecturer in a Federal Polytechnic.
He even invited me for his birthday on the 28th June.
When I went there, they gave me a bottle of beer but I couldn't drink it;
I said that it was like poison and started to vomit.
Since that day up to today, I have not tasted alcohol.
I hate it.
Let us clap for Jesus one more time.
- You mean God Almighty has set you free?
- Really, really!
Because Jesus Christ rose from the dead for you to receive a new instruction,
a new life, a new health, new finances, a new marriage.
We thank God Almighty that God Almighty has indeed fulfilled your heart's desire, in Jesus' name.
Now, let us hear from your wife.
Emmanuel!

My husband was addicted to drinking alcohol before we married.
Even before you got married to your husband, he had been drinking alcohol?
Yes.
Ever since this miracle has come upon your husband,

Now we are together; before, we were not praying but now,

Whatever I request from him, he gives me.
I thank God!

Yes. A Christian home does not just happen - it is built on the foundation of Jesus Christ.
People of God, it is really a miracle.
If you have run out of rope, grab onto faith.
The Lord will locate you as He located me, in Jesus' name.
I want to advise my friends who were in this problem with me - it is dangerous.
It destroyed my life before; I had a big stomach.
You would think it was because of money but it was because of beer, not money.
You can see my stomach is normal, now.
I am OK now and am feeling good.
You are perfectly fit, for the glory of God!
Let us clap for Jesus Christ!
We thank God Almighty.
Viewers all over the world,
Emmanuel TV is also a medium; keep watching Emmanuel TV.
One day, God will visit you.

Now that you have a new life, this new life gives you a new love.
Enforce this love into your life and God Almighty will back you up
by directing your steps, and this blessing shall remain permanent in your life, in Jesus' name.
