My name is Yusuf Hassan
I am from Adamawa State, Nigeria.
I worked with the National Drug Law Enforcement Agency (NDLEA).
In 1996, I was among the team of officers from NDLEA that came to
The Synagogue, Church Of All Nations to arrest Prophet T.B. Joshua.
There were 18 of us and six soldiers.
Some of us went to the old site of the church while some came here.
I was among those that came here. I took a position outside with a gun.
We were all armed.
I stood at the entrance gate of the church.
I was in charge of that gate.
Any vehicle coming in was allowed; any vehicle going out was not allowed.
We were supposed to come the Sunday before the Sunday we came
but the person that submitted the petition that Prophet T.B. Joshua
deals on drugs, told us that the Prophet would be on a pilgrimage that Sunday.
On Friday, the man came to the office and told us that the man of God was back.
We then prepared and came here to arrest Prophet T.B. Joshua.
We came, arrested the man of God and left with him that Sunday morning.
On our way to the office we asked him if he was a man of God.
Let him disappear if he's powerful.
When we got to our office we took his statement.
We searched the church premises but didn't find any drugs.
At the old site, our officers destroyed a lot of things while searching for the drugs
but we couldn't find anything.
Investigation was carried out; for 13 days he was there.
On the 13th day he was released because nothing incriminating
was found on him or with him, according to the investigation.
So, he was let go.
Among the officers that came to arrest Prophet T.B. Joshua then,
more than 3 of them are no longer alive
and the 18 officers, except for myself, have been dismissed.
I am on suspension.
I was charged to court and detained in Kirikiri for 10 months before I was granted bail.
But the case is still pending in federal high court until today.
How did you come to believe that the man of God is dealing with drugs?
It was a petition someone wrote to our chairman.
He said that the man in The Synagogue habours people and deals drugs.
That was why the General ordered Prophet T.B. Joshua's arrest.
I want Prophet T.B. Joshua to forgive me and ask God to deliver me from
the participation I took in his arrest.
I want the man of God to pray for me.
Falsely accused of drug dealing by the (NDLEA) National Drug Law Enforcement Agency,
Prophet T.B. Joshua was arrested and held in cell for 13 days.
This is the first appearance of Prophet T.B. Joshua,
having just been released from the cell.
You can see the man of God, Prophet T.B. Joshua looking lean, having spent 13 days in the cell.
I am using this opportunity to thank you all for your unequal efforts towards my absence.
God Almighty will continue to be with you.
If you say to yourself, "Why me of all this trouble, persecution,
tribulation and all sorts of things," I want you to now think back and ask,
"Why me of all these spiritual blessings in my life?"
When you count the blessing of God in your life, doubt will fly away.
Even if you are suffering and you lack of this and that, remember
the blessings of God like food, shelter and many others you are enjoying today.
Having searched and found nothing against him,
Prophet T.B. Joshua was released from the cell
and the following Sunday appeared in the church service
to thank the members and appreciate their love and support during his absence.
TT ANONYMOUS + Robert Begier 03/11/2019
