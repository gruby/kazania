This young boy came to The SCOAN with a horrific skin disease,
which had plagued his life for the past eight years affecting all his extremities.
The disease had spread its gruesome grip over the palms of his hands and across his fingers,
attacking the skin and flesh of his hands, giving them a horrible, fungus-covered appearance,
which was both extremely irritant and unbearably painful.
It had also extended to the back of his hands with infection and sores.
Moving down his body following the flow of blood,
this terrible virus had broken the skin of both legs in several places
and caused the affected areas to become dry, shiny and itchy.
But the worst sight is found on the boy's feet,
where the disease increased it's fetid hold.
The entirety of both his feet are smothered in this ugly, destructive fungal infection,
from the tips of his toes to the soles of his feet.
The raw pinkish flesh peeks out behind the dreadful imprisonment under the disease.
The appearance of this boy's feet is so alarming
and serves to remind us that satan's sicknesses and diseases are thieves of joy,
time, effort, energy and material.
The cause of such afflictions are nothing other than evil spirits, demons and devils.
For this purpose the Son of God, Jesus Christ was made manifest
to destroy the works of the devil.
Let us hear from the young man.
My name is Ugan Alphonsus, I'm 15 years old.
I am from Cross River State, Nigeria.
I have had a skin disease for eight years now.
It has created a lot of pain for me.
I find it hard to study in school because of the pain.
Sometimes I cannot sleep at night and it itches a lot.
So, it distracts me from other things that I have to do in the day.
I cannot walk well and I cannot go out.
I have been to many hospitals, medicine homes and herbalists but no solution.
I believe that the Lord God Almighty is going to heal me from this skin disease forever.
Be healed, in Jesus' name!
Thank You, Lord.
Mr Alphonsus Ugan was taken to The SCOAN prayer line,
where he received prayer from wise man Daniel, in the name of Jesus Christ.
A short time later, Mr Alphonsus came back to The SCOAN
to testify of the miraculous healing our Lord Jesus Christ had done in his life.
Everything about his skin has completely changed
and now glows with radiant good health.
The infection on the palms of his hands has totally dried up
and the sores are healed on both his palms and the backs of his hands and his fingers.
All the dry, itchy skin on his legs has gone, leaving only one mark on his right leg
where the sores used to be.
But best of all, his feet, which once looked so wrinkled and deformed
have been transformed back to the original position, in which God made them,
after the prayer of wise man Daniel, in the name of the Lord Jesus Christ.
Comparing his toes, you can see on the left side of the screen the nightmarish condition he came in,
and on the right side the glorious work of the Master Healer, Jesus Christ,
who levelled the mountain of sickness and disease in this boy's life, for His name sake.
The Bible says the resurrection power of Jesus Christ is transmitted to His followers,
giving them a glorious new existence.
It is this resurrection power that was transmitted through the prayer of the wise man
that has set this boy free, and free indeed.
The soles of his feet bear only a few telltale signs,
as the old skin is peeling away and the new healthy, smooth skin is coming through.
The skin and flesh of his toes are back to normal
and are clear of any of the hideous disease that once clung to them.
What is left to say, other than "Thank You, Jesus Christ"?
The evidence of Jesus Christ is lives changed.
This is evidence; his life has changed and to Jesus Christ be all the glory.
Hallelujah!
