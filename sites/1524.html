My name is Eugene Akwuwayo-Kwachi.
I am 46 years old.
I am a Ghanaian.
Based on the things that I heard about The SCOAN, that
Prophet T.B. Joshua was an ordinary human being
and he entered his room for seven good days and he came out to be a prophet,
that he lived in a secluded area where nobody touches him - under water.
All the powers he used are by demonic means.
With this, I harboured a deep-seated hatred for the man of God and The SCOAN as a whole.
Along the line, one of my house fellowship members
stopped coming to the church as well as the house.
So, one day I made enquiries about it and I sent one of my members,
those who are very good, to go and enquire about this brother.
So, when I came back, I was told that the brother was now at The SCOAN.
So, with this, I organised a prayer meeting in my house fellowship, three of us.
We prayed seriously for the brother.
Even upon that, my church organised a 40 day all night vigil.
Within this, we were praying about all kinds of demonic churches, The SCOAN inclusive.
We used to pray, "In the name of Jesus! In the mighty name of Jesus!
In the name of Jesus!
The blood of Jesus!
The precious blood of Jesus!
We release the brimstone of fire to The SCOAN, to dissolve all the power that they use in the church
and to bring back our brothers, those who are confused,
that the Lord Almighty should open the eyes of their understanding."
As we were saying this, even our church, where we used to have
double services in the morning and afternoon, you see that even our members are still dwindling.
So, with this, it became a problem.
I also heard along the line that the man of God, Pastor T.B. Joshua, travelled to Ghana
to destroy my people, my own fatherland.
In fact, I became very mad.
I had a hatred in me.
I began to pray.
I said, "Oh God. This is the time for You to show Your powers.
For Elijah prayed and fire came down to consume his enemies.
So whatever the man of God went to Ghana to do, let the fire destroy all his powers."
In fact, I didn't know that I was doing it to myself.
Problems started coming my way.
On the following weekend, that was Sunday, my 9 years old daughter called me,
"Daddy, let's go to The SCOAN."
I nearly chopped off my daughter's head.
I said, "What do you know about The SCOAN?"
"What do you want to do about The SCOAN?" I left her.
I didn't mind her.
I went on my way.
I have harboured a doubt about the church.
I have prayed seriously, negatively, concerning the man of God and The SCOAN as a whole.
It was ignorance.
Little did I know, even the brother who left my house fellowship, I went to the brother again.
Before you know it, the brother had hired a shop and started a barbing salon with a generator.
I said, "Yes, this boy has received demon power, so why won't he be rich?"
A brother that I know doesn't know anything but has become prosperous?
I said, "Yes, this boy has received demon power.
So automatically, he has become rich."
This made me to pray all the more with my members against the church.
Little did I know that I was doing all this to myself.
A lot of atrocities have gone out of my life.
I have done a lot of things.
I practised occultism, Buddhism.
In a nutshell, I am a fornicator; I am an adulterer.
TT Robert Begier 06/07/2020
