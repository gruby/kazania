AN UNUSUAL BEGINNING
God's way of doing things is so mysterious.
You have to be very careful.
Even where you are working, no matter what happens.
Either they cheat you, they demote you - don't quit because of that.
You need to ask God the direction.
They may demote you. They may take the junior one and promote them above you.
They may cut your salary in half.
Don't, because of all this, quit.
You need to examine what is happening.
God can use any foolish thing to address you.
When I say 'foolish thing' - these are the bad things.
God can use any foolish thing to try to address you.
I have some good examples.
This Emmanuel TV here in Nigeria, I want to tell you a story.
When I was airing my programme on some channels
- I don't want to mention their names; even one of the national channels -
because of me, they banned miracles in Nigeria.
Just because of me; not because of anybody else.
And they told me that miracles were banned.
"How will I show my programme?"
They said, "Just talk".
Jesus is Lord.
I removed all my programmes from all the stations and I went to the mountain to pray.
God said, "Yes, I am aware. I want you to discover yourself. I will give you a channel called Emmanuel TV".
Emmanuel TV came.
The name 'Emmanuel' is too long. How will I write the whole 'Emmanuel TV'?.
And I looked at all the channels. It was either three or four letters,
but my own would be so long.
Can you see? That is human.
And God said 'Emmanuel TV'.
I now went to register 'SCOAN TV'.
The first month they were showing this. The channel was called SCOAN before.
The first month, second month.
In a vision I was being warned that "When you wake up, change this name to Emmanuel TV".
This was the instruction from God.
