THE MAIN THING ABOUT CHRISTIANITY: TB Joshua
Be all God has called you.
If you want to be all God has called you to be,
you have got to spend time with Him
and learn from Him, at His feet.
The main thing about Christianity is not what we do.
"In Jesus' name" - you spend all your time fasting, praying - fine.
You preach and teach every day; they send you from one place to another - it is good, fine.
You pay your tithes - fine.
You sleep in the church - fine.
All this - fine.
It is not what we do but the relationship we maintain
and the atmosphere produced by that relationship.
If you say you have a relationship with God,
that relationship must definitely produce an atmosphere.
What we do for God is only a reflection of the relationship we have with Him.
Satan hates an independent mind, the mind that would think for itself.
Satan hates an independent mind,
the mind that is determined to find out from God the truth.
What are you saying?
He is a good listener; he will listen to you but he will still find out from God before taking action.
Satan would want you to take all you hear without question.
Don't be afraid to say, "I don't know" if you don't know.
If you know, say, "I know".
That is the mind of Christ.
The presence of God comes by relationship with Him.
Work on your relationship with Him.
Stop deceiving yourself, "I'm a pastor, this, that" and wasting of your time, sleeping in the church;
it is wasting of effort, let us work on our relationship.
Tell your neighbour, "Work on your relationship".
And stop deceiving yourself, "I'm a Christian, pastor, prophet, bishop, evangelist,
counselor, deacon, deaconess".
Go and work on your relationship with Him.
Yes.
Because all these titles cannot take us to Heaven.
"I'm a prophet"; on the last day God will not say, "T.B. Joshua, he is a prophet - allow him to enter".
Which kind of prophet?
It is relationship with Him that will take you to Heaven.
All these, "I'm a pastor, prophet, deacon, deaconess, Christian, this, that" are symbolic.
Jesus - the hope of glory.
Work on your relationship.
The atmosphere produced by that relationship will usher you to Jesus.
Be all God has called you.
