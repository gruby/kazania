During the Monday Live Service at The SCOAN
Prophet TB Joshua prays for the international visitors, in the power of the Holy Spirit.
Among those who received a healing touch from our Lord Jesus Christ
was this man who had suffered from deafness for five years.
After the Monday Service where the man of God prayed for this man
who was one of the international visitors to The SCOAN,
one of his roommates came to call the camera crew
to listen to his wonderful testimony.
The camera crew rush up the stairs to this man's room
and as the doors are opened
they are welcomed by the excited roommates
who share in this man's joy at his healing.
Let's listen to him.
I'm totally delivered! I am healed! I'm saved!
My name is John Marufu.
I am a Zimbabwean, but I am residing in Botswana.
Five years ago I went to hospital - one of the biggest hospitals in Botswana where I am staying.
I had deafness of the left ear; it was totally closed.
I had lost hope. It affected my life and I was going through depression. I did not understand myself.
I said to myself, "How can I, as young as I am, be going deaf?".
For example, if you are in an interview and you keep saying, "Huh?".
The person will end up losing confidence in you.
Even if you go for business meetings, people will end up losing confidence in you
if you keep on asking them the same thing.
I was saying to myself that after the service, I would visit the doctor for ear cleaning.
I had lost hope completely.
I came here to The SCOAN and the man of God prayed for me.
From there, we went to the dining room and then to our room.
I went to my bed to relax and I tried to sleep, but I could not.
I was feeling so hot.
All of a sudden, my left ear started itching; it was so hot inside.
So, I woke up and went to the dining room to fetch cold water to cool myself down because I was so hot.
I tried to sleep, but I could not.
I took my ear bud and started to clean the ear and then something came out.
All of a sudden I heard a 'shhhh' sound. Then I started hearing!
I closed my right ear and found that I could hear.
My name is Kudakwashe Nzvengende from Zimbabwe, but I currently reside in Botswana.
I want to confirm the healing of John Marufu.
In 2008, I came to Botswana and joined a company where he was working.
After six months working together, John Marufu was given three weeks off
because of his deafness.
The Managing Director almost wanted to fire him because he was always ill or off work
because of the problem of deafness.
So, I am happy that he can now hear with both ears.
My name is Dr David Maruma. I am from Botswana.
I am one of the people who witnessed a miracle when Mr Marufu was healed.
I heard him shout that he had been healed of deafness of the left ear.
I came in and listened to what he was saying and started praising God.
Deafness may be due to many things; it could be trauma or an infection.
Whatever was the cause, we are happy that he has been healed.

TESTIMONY TIME
Praise the Lord!
Emmanuel
(God With Us - Matthew 1:23)
Let me start by introducing myself.
My name is John Marufu.
I am a Zimbabwean, but I am staying in Botswana.
I have been suffering from deafness from an ear problem for five years.
I couldn't hear in my left ear.
So, during a Monday service, the man of God prayed for me.
After praying for me, I went down.
He just touched me and I went down.
Then I stood up and the ushers told me to go back to the dining room or to my room.
It is done; it is okay.
But I started fighting with them saying, "Do you think I came all this way to go and eat at the dining room?".
"No, I am not going. I want to see the man of God!".
This unbelieving spirit was upon me.
I was not satisfied with just a touch.
After most of the people had been prayed for, I forced myself to go out.
I went to the dining room. Then, later to my room.
When I got to the room, I tried to sleep but I felt heat all over my body. It felt like fire.
Then my left ear started to itch and became hot inside.
I climbed off my bed and went back to the dining room to drink some water in an attempt to recover.
I went back to the room and I could still not sleep.
So, I tried to read my Bible, but I failed because the heat inside my ear was too much for me;
the fire was too much on this ear.
So, I took my ear bud and began cleaning my left ear.
I used a second earbud and started cleaning it.
Then all of a sudden, I just heard 'shhhh'. Then, I was healed!
I blocked my right ear to prove it to myself.
I heard the men talking in my room.
I woke my roommate who was next to me and said to him,
"My brother, mine is done. I am totally healed. I am delivered. I can hear!"
"I suffered with this problem for over five years, but now I am healed!".
I told him that I had booked an appointment with my doctor upon my return from Nigeria to have my ears cleaned.
But now I can hear perfectly. I am okay.
I want to thank God!
God touched my ear, which was not the problem why I came to The SCOAN.
DEAF-DEFYING MIRACLE!!!
I want to thank God!
Children of God, imagine because of this deafness I almost got fired from my job,
and at times I lost big tenders because people did not take me seriously
when I kept having to ask, "Hey, hey, pardon?".
People did not take me seriously.
Imagine going into a business meeting with senior people and you are trying to get a big tender
and you keep saying, "Huh?".
They won't believe in you.
They will say, "No, this man is trying to play with us".
So, I want to thank God. God has highly favoured me!
My advice is that when the man of God says that it is done, it will surely be done.
There is no two ways about it.
When he prayed for me, it was totally done, despite my unbelieving heart.
And I received it.
Glory be to God!
Wow. Viewers all over the world, listen to that.
