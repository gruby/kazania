Pray Along With Prophet T.B. Joshua
Everywhere You Go
Viewers all over the world, prepare your hearts to receive
and get ready to pray along with Prophet T.B. Joshua.
Whatever situation you are in, God Almighty will take you from glory to glory.
Pray along with faith, in Jesus' name.
In the mighty name of Jesus Christ!
I want to specially pray for those who are sick
and you believe what you are facing is beyond you.
You are not going to sleep on the bed of sickness today.
Let us go. You evil spirit, you must listen!
I say, listen, in the name of Jesus!
You, evil spirit, listen, in the name of Jesus!
The cause of infirmity! You are the cause of infirmity.
You are the cause of setback, limitation in progress,
disease, sickness, failure and yoke.
You, evil spirit, listen, in the name of Jesus!
It must leave you because you belong to Jesus.
I belong to Jesus.
There is no more condemnation.
There is no condemnation.
There is no condemnation, no guilt.
I belong to Jesus.
You, evil spirit, listen, in the name of Jesus!
I command you, out of my business!
You, evil spirit, listen, in the name of Jesus!
It must listen because you belong to Jesus.
It must listen because I belong to Jesus.
No more condemnation.
No more guilt.
It must listen; I belong to Jesus!
I belong to Jesus!
It must listen, in the name of Jesus!
As you continue to offer this prayer in your home, church, household,
it is prayer God has given to you; offer it.
I belong to Jesus; no more condemnation.
Viewers all over the world,
you belong to Jesus; there is no more condemnation.
You belong to Jesus; there is no more guilt.
Evil spirit is behind those things -
failure, setback, limitation in progress.
I say to you, you belong to Jesus; no more condemnation.
Evil spirit, listen! In the name of Jesus, I command you out!
In the name of Jesus Christ!
Your word will not go without results.
Open your lips and say after me,
I belong to Jesus; no more condemnation.
No more condemnation for those who belong to Jesus.
No more guilt.
You, evil spirit, the author of failure,
the author of sickness,
the author of disease,
the author of fear,
I command you, in the name of Jesus,
I command that sickness, I command that disease.
Viewers all over the world,
where can you go from His presence?
From East, North, South, West, all over,
whatever position you are in,
whatever sickness or disease you are facing,
listen to me those of you that hear my voice.
This is the voice of God.
I declare war over your disease and sickness!
This is battle!
I declare battle over your sickness and disease!
I say you, disease and sickness, you, infirmity,
whatever disease, whatever sickness within your blood or fluid,
I command it out, in the name of Jesus!
Just begin to touch those areas you feel pain,
Are you on the sickbed? Are you in the wheelchair?
Fibroid, cancer, ulcer, hypertension, diabetes,
no matter the name you call it,
never a sickness or disease Jesus cannot heal;
never a disease Jesus cannot cure.
Be cured, in the name of Jesus!
Say: You, only, can heal!
I can't hear you.
Be cured, in the name of Jesus!
That disease in your blood, that sickness in your bones,
that sickness in your flesh, that disease in your tendon,
Father, thank You for the cure.
Father, thank You for Your healing.
Father, thank you for Your touch.
As you are giving thanks to God,
whatever disease, whatever sickness you are having,
you, dry bone, I command you, right now, to rise again, in the name of Jesus!
I can see you being cured, in the name of Jesus!
I can see you being healed, in the name of Jesus!
I can see you being blessed, in the name of Jesus!
When I say, I can see you being blessed, say, "I am blessed!"
When I say, I can see you being cured, say, "I am cured!"
I can see you being cured, in the name of Jesus!
I can see you being healed, in the name of Jesus!
I can see you being blessed, in the name of Jesus!
Check yourself.
Nothing is impossible to the Word of God.
Tell your neighbour: Nothing is impossible to the Word of God.
He cures, He heals, He blesses!
Breakthrough!
When you speak the Word of God, nothing is impossible.
