355 - T.B. Joshua's Secrets REVEALED!!!


I believe each one of us should challenge ourselves to go in the level that Prophet T.B Joshua is flowing.
In my congregation, I have had guests
from Columbia and other countries.
We have received guests from other nations, from Columbia and other nations of the world in our church.
But it is the very first time
in our ministerial lives
that we have picked up the guest from the
airport and not taken them to a hotel.
Instead, we took him from the airport
to a prayer mountain.
It is the very first time
in my ministerial life,
that I have seen such a thing.
We took Prophet T.B Joshua to a mountain
because he asked to stay in a mountain.
We did not take him to a hotel.
We took him to a mountain.
I am shocked, it is something new for me.
It immediately reminded me of the Prophet Elijah.
When they couldn't find him, they found him in the mountains.
It also made me remember John the Baptist.
He dressed very humbly, and he lived very far in the mountains.
I believe that his own life
gives us a message.
We, as pastors, find ourselves in a
lot of activities in our lives,
and sometimes we abandon what we should never abandon -
our primary relationship is the Lord and us.
Columbia Pastor's Conference
with Prophet T.B Joshua
We assigned bodyguards to the Prophet, but he did not want them.
In these days, we understand why he did not need them.
He came one day
and the next day at 3 am in the morning.
He was in the mountain, but he climbed to a higher place in the mountain at 3 am in the morning.
The bodyguards had to climb to the higher place at that time in the cold.
The bodyguards were asking,
"What's wrong with the prophet?"
"He is gong to make us ill!"
His lifestyle is very different
from the majority of us.
Maybe I am revealing a secret.
It is good for us as pastors to know what I am saying.
Let us find our own mountain, a place of prayer.
Let us find our exclusive
time with the Lord,
where there are no commercials,
interruptions, and nothing to disturb us.
Can you say with me, "I want my mountain!"
Tell your neighbor, "We need to have
our own mountain of prayer."
Glory to the Lord!
I have a lot of expectations today,
but I know that according to the desire of your heart,
you will receive from the Lord
by His Holy Spirit
through his Prophet.
How many of you will say "Amen"?
Give an applause and give the glory to God.
Columbia Pastor's Conference
with Prophet T.B Joshua
