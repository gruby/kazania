My name is Joy Bongos.
I am a Nigerian. I come from Kano.
The problem that brought me here to The SCOAN was a virus and the left-hand side of my chest.
For more than four years, I have been coughing - no sleeping, no solution.
I went to many places, to many doctors; yet, there was no solution.
I even went to traditionalistis to get herbal medicine.
I took it; yet, there was no solution.
To the extent that my sisters used to call me 'church prostitute'.
They called me 'church prostitute' because anywhere they said there was healing taking place,
I would rush there but yet, there was still no solution.
I suffered with this. If I was in public, I would be coughing, coughing - no control.
If I was in the church, I would be coughing; in fact I would not stop.
When I started coughing, I would hold my stomach and chest so that I could cough.
If I did not hold this area, I could not cough; it would be paining me.
So, last Sunday, I was here, sitting up there on the gallery.
The man of God was touching people down here.
I said, "Oh my God, why will the man of God not come up and touch me?"
People were telling me that I was funny.
After thirty minutes, I said, "Oh God, let the man of God come up".
I sat down but the Spirit came to me and told me that I should claim it.
So whenever the man of God was healing people, I would be claiming, "I am healed".
Whenever he touched someone - whether it was the same sickness I had or not, I would say, "I am healed".
During the Mass Prayer he started praying for people.
Then he said that any thing that was in our liver, we should vomit it out.
I was sitting there; I felt dizzy and then I started vomiting.
I started vomiting where I sat.
Then the ushers came and told me, "Come here and vomit".
That was how I got recorded.
When I was vomiting, I didn't even know that I was vomiting blood.
All of a sudden, I just vomited blood; I don't know what happened.
Let me tell you in short, my dear brethren:
When I was vomiting, it was like someone was using a broom to sweep my heart.
Everywhere was just sweeping; I was just continuously vomiting blood.
When the man of God asked me to sleep, I didn't know I fell asleep.
When I was sleeping, something was going out of body; it was just moving in my body.
I started stretching my hand and turned and after I turned, I coughed and vomited again.
Some blood also came out after the man of God had asked me to sleep.
That very Sunday, I went back home.
Before, I could not sleep without rubbing garlic on my body.
If I did not rub garlic on my body, I would feel cold and start coughing.
I also kept water beside me 24/7.
That night, I also kept water beside me in case I would vomit or cough.
But I slept like a baby - I didn't cough, I didn't rub the garlic;
I didn't do anything; I just slept till daybreak.
When I woke up, I said, "The God of T.B. Joshua has done it. Finally, I will not cough again".
