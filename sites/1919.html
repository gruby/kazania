TESTIMONY CONTINUES
My name is Maiki Ohashi.
I am from Japan.
I want to thank God for delivering me from my addiction.
I was a chain-smoker; I used to smoke a lot of cigarettes.
I could not think well.
Every time I was doing something, I would make mistakes.
It affected me very badly.
At what age did you start smoking?
I started smoking when I was 14 years old.
I just tasted it and from that day, I always wanted to smoke every day.
I used to smoke three packs a day.
And each pack contained how many sticks?
Twenty sticks.
So you would be smoking sixty sticks of cigarettes every day?
Yes.
For how many years?
It all started in 2010 - when I started chain-smoking.
I would always cough so hard.
My mind was always thinking.
I always made mistakes in my job and at home.
In 2014, I touched the screen and prayed along with T.B. Joshua in our house.
When I touched the screen, immediately, my arms became stiff.
I didn't know why but I couldn't move my arms for about 30 minutes.
Afterwards, my arms became normal and I fell asleep.
When I woke up, I tried smoking but the taste was not good anymore.
I didn't like smoking anymore.
Let's put our hands together for Jesus Christ!
I threw all my cigarettes in the trash can.
So, since that day, you threw away all the cigarettes you had in your house,
and since that day, you stopped smoking completely?
Yes.
Hallelujah!
After that, do you still have the urge to smoke?
No. Even if I am in an environment where people are smoking,
I don't have the urge to smoke anymore.
Hallelujah!
At my place of work, they were confused at what had happened to me.
I could work and think well.
It boosted my confidence at work and they have trust in me now.
Hallelujah!
Let's put our hands together for Jesus Christ!
My advice to people: We cannot stop an addiction on our own.
We have to go straight to Jesus Christ
and ask for the Holy Spirit to deliver us from all addictions in our lives.
Only Jesus can heal us.
Hallelujah! Let's put our hands together for Jesus!
Yes, indeed, wonderful advice: Only Jesus can.
Brother, we thank God Almighty for your life
and we pray that God will give you the grace to maintain this wonderful deliverance
that God has done in your life,
by making God's Word the standard for your life, in Jesus' name.
TT Robert Begier 17/07/2020
RV Gary Tonge 17/07/2020
