Mr Odigie is brought in a car by his family to The SCOAN.
He suffers from inability to walk,
lying flat in a car because of excruciating pain caused by a spinal cord injury.
You can see the expression of agony on the man's face,
as they carry him out of the car into the wheelchair.
In deep sorrow and distress, the man's family wheel him to the emergency section of The SCOAN,
looking in faith unto our Lord Jesus Christ to have mercy and heal their brother.
Jesus never says no to those who ask for healing, according to His Word
because His will for us is to be physically healed and spiritually saved.
God's power and effectiveness have not changed.
Let's hear from Mr John Odigie.

Jesus is the same yesterday, today and forever.
His power and effectiveness are even greater today

Healing is taking place.
As it was, so it is; Jesus Christ healed the sick and still heals today.
The man of God, Prophet T.B. Joshua is praying for Mr Odigie,

TESTIMONY CONTINUES
Filled with ease and joy, Mr John Odigie stands to his feet
and moves in to position to share his incredible testimony.
The wheelchair he once depended on has become part of his history.
My name is John Odigie.
On Wednesday, I came here with paralysis of the body.
On that day, I could not walk, move; I had pain all over my body - waist, stomach.
In fact, I could not move my legs.
When I was brought here on that day,
I was brought before the man of God, Prophet T.B. Joshua at the prayer line.
He came to me, held my back that was in pain and as he was touching me,
the pain started going away gradually until he told me to stand up and I started to walk.
I don't have any pain in my body.
I can walk properly now, as you can see.
My name is Mrs Ize Iyamu.
The man here is my younger brother.
I will thank God and we thank Prophet T.B. Joshua for saving us from all these problems.
I was very happy that day and I really cried.
Tears of joy came out of my eyes.
I will live my life as a good Christian.
I promise to serve God and sin no more.
