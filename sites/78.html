you have been praying, fasting -

through Christ Jesus who strengthens you.

in the name of Jesus!

anoints those who He chooses to anoint.
He chose you today!
You are a chosen one!
I don't deserve it;
He decided to choose me.
He chooses whom He chooses,
He loves whom He loves.
I am anointed today,
in the name of Jesus!
