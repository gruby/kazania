Emmanuel
(God With Us - Matthew 1:23)
My name is Lucy Onoriode Aghri.
I am here to share my testimony of the wondrous deliverance that God has done for me
via the New Morning Water.
Two weeks ago I was very lucky and opportuned to be in the service
when the man of God shared the New Morning Water - the power and the glory of God.
Prior to that time in March 2017, just a few days after I relocated from Abuja,
I started having excruciating pains in my womb.
I could not explain the pains - the pains were such that when they started I would not be able to walk.
So, I had so much fear.
And at the same time, I was eating in my dream
and drinking all sorts of unknown liquid substances in my dream night after night, about three times a week.
These pains would come and I would drink water. I would not know what to do.
I went to a HealthPlus Pharmacy and they gave me some medication.
I took three courses of antibiotics, but the pains continued without ceasing.
At that point I heard different stories.
Some said that the water in Lagos was not good,
but that was not the case.
I went to the Gold Cross Hospital in Ikoyi when the pains became too much to bear.
They carried out tests and a scan and they could not find anything wrong with me.
At that point I knew the devil was after my life and I had to do something about it.
As I said earlier, I was in The SCOAN two weeks ago
and the evangelists sprayed the New Morning Water on me.
Three days later I got back from work in the evening about 6 pm,
and the pains started and this time it was worse.
I was wondering what was going on in my life. I did not know what to do.
I drank two bottles of water and the pains were excruciating.
I fetched the New Morning Water and I sprayed it on my womb and the pain was even worse than before.
It was like labour pain. At that point I was losing my breath.
So, I quickly called a friend of mine and said, "Something is about to come out through my body".
"I have a lot of pressure. Something is about to come out of my body".
And she said, "Are you pregnant?". I said, "I know that I am not pregnant".
She said, "Yes, we know. But what is coming out? Where is it coming out from?"
And I was panicking. I said, "I don't know".
It felt as if there was a 5 kilogram stone in my stomach.
At that point I could not move.
I just managed to get myself to my bathroom
and when I got there, I could not talk on the phone.
All of a sudden, I passed out a long substance into the bathroom
and immediately when it came out of my body the pain just stopped.
Shall we put our hands together for Jesus?
SUPERNATURAL Woman 'Gives Birth' To Worm!!!
As you can see here, this is the long substance that came out of my body.
I don't know what it is; I cannot explain it.
But I can tell you that immediately when it came out of my body - it was just like a woman that has just given birth -
the pains stopped and up to this moment I have had no more pains.
Before now I would feel as though something was eating deeply into my womb.
I would hear voices that would say, "This could be cancer", and I would rebuke it.
At that point, when I started hearing voices too, I knew that the devil was after my life.
I just give God the glory because I don't know what would have happened if this stayed in my body.
Shall we put our hands together for Jesus?
Hallelujah!
We give glory to God Almighty. Only Jesus could have justly done this.
You heard the testimony of the sister who said
there was no hospital she did not visit in order to find out what was the cause of this problem.
But nothing could be detected. She said she had a terrible evil attack whereby she would eat daily in the dream
and she realised that this was spiritual and only Jesus could rescue her.
Imagine this object in her womb - we are just seeing it like this -
when it was still in her womb, you can imagine what would be there -
eating up her womb and leading her quietly to her grave, as she said.
But the moment she was ministered the New Morning Water, as the man of God introduced it two weeks ago,
she delivered this object.
And since then?
The pains have completely gone.
Can you press on your stomach and tell us how you are feeling?
I don't have any pain there anymore.
Could you do this before?
No, it even distracted me at work.
When I tried to carry on with my daily routine, I would feel the pain and it caused me a lot of embarrassment.
Press it once again and tell us.
No, I don't feel any pain.
Put your hands together once again for Jesus.
Hallelujah! We give glory to God for this.
TT Rae van Zyl 20/11/2020
