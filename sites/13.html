DON'T GIVE UP! | Viewers Prayer with TB Joshua
Viewers, it's time to pray with Prophet T.B Joshua
and take your place in the believer's authority to break the grip and hold of the devil over your life.
Anywhere you are in the world,
place your hands wherever you have pain or discomfort or whatever constant experience
you have been having and let the precious blood of Jesus Christ wash it away right now.
Now is the time to restore every breakthrough key which sin has stolen from you.
As the prophet prays for you begin to experience the joy of salvation
and receive the breakthrough keys to your good health, your marital life, your finances, your business, career
and your destiny in the name of Jesus Christ. Your challenge, if it is allowed by God as a trial -
do not see it strange and begin to doubt your God, instead count it all joy that your opportunity for victory
is at hand. As you pray with prophet T.B Joshua right now, you will see that
God's power is so real, so authentic and so forceful that it cannot be limited by space and time.
Viewers all over the world, be encouraged, when God corrects you.
Whatever your challenges are,
your challenges could be sickness, poverty, backwardness,
back luck, rejection, limitation in progress, marital problem -
I don't know, God knows.
Whatever your challenges are, are a way of correction, if you are a Christian.
Don't give up! If you are a Christian, don't give up.
I want you to see it as a reason for believing God,
just as you see your good time.
See your pain as a reason for believing God,
just as you see when money is in your pockets,
just as you see when food is on the table.
See it, if you are a Christian!
Whatever experience you are having now, it is time to put an end to it.
Our God gives good health when we are sick.
Our God gives blessing, when we are poor.
Our God gives freedom.
Right now, are you ready?
Every constant experience of poverty, sickness, disease, backwardness, bad luck, limitation in progress -
right now in your life, be washed away by the Blood of Jesus!
Be flushed out by the Blood of Jesus!
In the name of Jesus Christ!
Viewers all over the world, flush them out!
Every constant experience of limitation, backwardness, rejection, marital problems -
right now in your life, be flushed out by the Blood of Jesus!
Begin to flush them out.
Begin to wash them away.
In the name of Jesus Christ!
TT Pastor Daniel Yeboah
