Prophet T.B. Joshua is the General Overseer of The SCOAN.
T.B. Joshua himself says that the Founder is Jesus Christ.
He is just one who has submitted himself humbly to the will of God.
He was born on June 12, 1963 in Ondo State, Nigeria.
The journey of T.B. Joshua is a history of how God raised up a humble man to be the director of an international ministry.
This ministry attracts people from all over the world just to see the reality of the power of God.
From dropping out of secondary school in his first year
to working in a poultry farm; from teaching little children while attending evening classes
to washing people's legs on the muddy streets of Lagos;
from a forty day fast to receiving a divine call
and starting a ministry with mere eight members -
the life of T.B. Joshua is a story of amazing grace and unwavering focus.
Today, he is a mentor to presidents yet a friend to the widows and less privileged,
a role model to his generation
yet T.B. Joshua is a humble and hardworking man,
toiling tirelessly for the advancement of God's kingdom.
His story is an encouragement that there is hope for the weak.
For us, as a Church in the city of Cali,
it is a privilege and honour to introduce Prophet T.B. Joshua,
for the first time in Colombia and in the entire Western hemisphere.
A big round of applause to our God
for His servant, Prophet T.B. Joshua.
