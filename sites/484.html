My name is Ikenna Nnebor.
I am from Anambra State but I reside in Cameroon.
The problem that brought me to The SCOAN was business failure.

Prophet T.B. Joshua told me to come.
There was water there so he asked me to go through it and come to him.
I said no because I would fall inside the water.
He told me to come.
I wanted to hold onto something but fell, yet the water was not deep.
When I reached T.B. Joshua, he started telling me many things.
