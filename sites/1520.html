Viewers all over the world, we know different positions -
we have people watching us right now on the sickbed, in the prison.
Wherever you are, the position of your heart matters.
What is the position of your heart?
Be released, in the name of Jesus!
Be ready to forgive.
"Forgive us as we forgive."
Forgive - you will be forgiven!
Be released, in the name of Jesus!
Release from poverty, release from affliction, release from setback, release from stagnation!
Be released, in the name of Jesus!
Are you on the sickbed?
Be released from that bed, in the name of Jesus!
Be released from that affliction, in the name of Jesus!
Give thanks to God for your release.
Give thanks to God for your freedom.
Every damaged organ, every damaged system - be restored, in the name of Jesus!
In Jesus Christ's name we pray!
I can't hear you.
[Amen.]
I can't hear you.
[Amen.]
Worship Him.
