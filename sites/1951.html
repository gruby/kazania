Get ready to hear this important statement from Prophet T.B. Joshua.
Good Morning And Win Today!
Following the announcement that church services will resume in Lagos on August 9th 2020 -
that is, this coming Sunday - I wish to commend the authorities for their efforts so far.
However, having heard from the authorities, we are now waiting to hear from God.
Please be assured that as soon as we hear from God, we will inform you when our services will resume.
Emmanuel!
(God With Us - Matthew 1:23)
TT Robert Begier 05/08/2020
