I FIND JESUS RELIABLE AND TRUSTWORTHY!
Prayer With T.B. Joshua
PRAYER TIME
Viewers at home, remember where you are coming from,
remember your beginning,
remember your blessing.
Confess this viewers: Oh Lord Jesus, I have tested You in my business.
Oh Lord Jesus, I have tested You in my career.
Oh Lord Jesus, I have tested You in my marriage.
Oh Lord Jesus, I have tested You in my health.
Oh Lord Jesus, I have tested You in my situation.
I find You reliable and trustworthy, oh Lord! Thank You!
In the name of Jesus.
