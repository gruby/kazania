Brain dead coma patient wakes up after TB Joshua's prayer in Nazareth!!!
Emmanuel! My name is Mrs Elvira Wi from Korea.
I have an incredible testimony to share to the glory of God!
I travelled from Korea to attend the Nazareth Meeting with TB Joshua.
I took with me the photograph of a friend named Sergey Pak who had been in a coma for 2 weeks.
He fell from a high roof while working for a company and suffered fractures all over his body.
After the fall, he was rushed to the hospital and declared clinically dead for one hour.
However, his relatives did not agree with the verdict of death and continued to proclaim life and pray.
Miraculously, he came to life but did not come to his senses. He was in a coma.
The doctors said that he would be like a 'vegetable' because his brain was for so long without oxygen.
They said he would be on a life support machine for the rest of his life...
So, in Nazareth, when I got into the prayer line with a photograph of Sergey, TB Joshua laid his hands on me.
At that moment, Sergey's father - Pastor Igor Pak from Kazakhstan - was watching the live service on Emmanuel TV!
When TB Joshua prayed for me, the sick son's father felt a force touch his own head!
The next day, Sergey regained consciousness!!!
Ever since, he has begun a remarkable road to recovery!
By God's grace, he can now speak freely and communicate with those around him!
I want to thank all those who prayed for me.
I also want to thank T.B. Joshua for praying for me in Nazareth!
All the doctors are shocked at this miraculous recovery!
Thank You, Jesus!!!
