Viewers all over the world,
at God's time healing happens,
deliverance happens
and salvation takes place.
It is God's time for your miracle!
Allow God's Word to live in you,
to get into your blood
and grow to gain supremacy over you
as you prepare your hearts
to join Prophet T.B. Joshua in prayer
to bring about the kind of conversion
that comes from the Living Word
and receive the breakthrough
that will make you forget the pain of the past,
in Jesus' name.
REBUKE THE SPIRT OF SICKNESS!!!
T.B. Joshua Viewers Prayer
Viewers all over the world, where can you go from His presence?
Every spirit troubling your life,
every spirit troubling your career,
every spirit troubling your marriage -
I rebuke them, in the name of Jesus!
Be rebuked, in the name of Jesus!
I command them out, in the name of Jesus!
Spirit of sickness,
spirit of disease,
spirit of infirmity,
spirit of poverty,
spirit of hardship,
spirit of barrenness -
be rebuked!
I command them out! Out!
REBUKE THE SPIRT OF SICKNESS!!!
T.B. Joshua Viewers Prayer
Viewers all over the world, you are with me here and I am with you there
by the power of the Holy Ghost!
Are you ready?
That affliction, evil spirits are behind it.
That affliction, infirmity, evil spirits are behind it.
That poverty, evil spirits are behind it.
That hardship, evil spirits are responsible.
Are you with me?
Every spirit troubling your career,
every spirit troubling your health,
every spirit troubling your marriage,
every spirit troubling your destiny -
I rebuke them, in the name of Jesus!
I cast them out, in the name of Jesus!
I command them out, in the name of Jesus!
Leave my people!
My God, can my people be sick people?
Can my people be poor people?
I say, leave my people!
You are my people!
Say to me, you are my people!
Say, I am your people!
You are my people!
We are people of God!
You evil spirit, leave my people!
Leave their lives!
Leave their business!
Leave their career!
Leave their marriage!
REBUKE THE SPIRT OF SICKNESS!!!
T.B. Joshua Viewers Prayer
This is the voice of God for you.

every spirit, every Goliath, every demon,
every spirit, every situation facing my business,
every situation facing my career,
every situation facing my destiny,

oh Lord, every spirit tormenting my life,
be disgraced and defeated,
