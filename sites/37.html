STANDARD FOR LIFE
Attention: The footage you are about to watch is of a sensitive nature
and although it is not suitable for all viewers,
it is for the glory of God.
Look, as this man is brought to The Synagogue, Church Of All Nations,
being fully carried by two men, due to the severity of his affliction,
the terrible leg ulcer is plain to see.
Unable to apply any pressure on the wounded leg, the man limps
and is totally dependant on the support of the two men.
They help him to his seat, with a great effort.
Mr. Onyeka Nnaji has come to The SCOAN with the problem of a horrific leg ulcer.
Affecting the entire leg, the ulcer has eaten away at the flesh of the calf muscle.
Gruesomely, the remaining skin hangs open, revealing the damage done to the inside of the leg.
The jagged face of the wound continually oozes pus, which drips down his leg with blood.
As one can barely behold the appalling sight,
so one can hardly imagine the offensive odor caused by the ulcer
which this man has had to live with in such pain.
Down the leg, you can see his ankle and foot are blistered and swollen,
a tell tale sign that the ulcer is getting worse.
A full on view from the back of his leg confronts one with the severity of this condition.
He is in dire need of the Healer, the Restorer and Repairer, Jesus Christ.
Welcome to the arena of liberty, The Synagogue, Church Of All Nations prayer line.
Father, we thank You for Your healing.
We thank You for the power of ressurection, in Jesus' name. Thank You, Lord.
He awaits his encounter with Prophet T.B. Joshua at the prayer line
Prophet T.B. Joshua has begun to pray for the leg ulcer of Mr. Onyeka Nnaji, in the name of Jesus Christ.
In the mighty name of Jesus Christ, you are free.
Healed In Jesus' Name
Glory be to God, Mr. Nnaji has been declared free and walks in the light of his testimony.
Before and After
Sometime after the prayer, Mr. Onyeka Nnaji came back to The SCOAN
to show how far his healing had progressed.
As he turned his leg around, we can see the color of his skin has become normal
and the skin around his ankles and toes has been healed.
Most importantly, the once raptured and grievous wound is showing good signs of recovery
and the presence of hair on his leg once again indicates that his good health has indeed been restored.
Before and After
Still later, Mr. Onyeka Nnaji returned to The SCOAN,
this time to testify to the goodness and power of God in his life.
As his leg, which was once smitten by a terrible ulcer, is now completely healed.
Turning his leg, you can still see the scar tissue, the last evidence of the once horrific and incurable condition
that brought Mr. Nnaji to The SCOAN,
an affliction which was cured by a word in the mighty name of Jesus Christ!
Before and After

How do you feel now?

Complimentary to his healing, Mr. Nnaji has gained weight and looks extremely well.
Mr. Nnaji boldly taps his leg without any fear of pain.
When asked if he still felt any pain, he replied with a resounding "No".
