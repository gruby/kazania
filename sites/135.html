Emmanuel!
(God With Us - Matthew 1:23)
Brother, you are very welcome today to the Anointing Water Revival in the UK.
Please tell us your name and whereabouts you are from.
My name is Chris Jansen.
I am originally from Sydney but I am living in Netherlands for two months now.
- Originally from Australia?
- Yes.
You are very welcome.
Can you tell us what were the problems that actually brought you here
to the Anointing Water Revival in the UK?
I had depression for many years of my life.
I haven't had any breakthroughs with my life, my situation,
with moving in the ministry or work.
I had a lot of suicidal impressions and impulsions in my mind.
I had a lot of torment mentally in my life.
How did this all begin?
It began with panic attacks.
I began to get anxiety when I was 12 and it continued till I was 25.
When those panic attacks and that depression comes, how does it really affect you,
what are the things they cause you to do?
It isolated me; I didn't have a social life.
It put me in a place of feeling hopeless and despaired.
In life, it was very difficult to move forward and find my way.
What about your career?
How has this affected your career?
Part of the reason why I came was to get breakthrough and my purpose in life
and find whether it is in ministry or career.
It had really affected my life in a major way.
As a result of all of these problems, how did you find people were treating you,
and the reactions of people towards you?
People can tend to turn off you because you are not feeling so fantastic within yourself
and it was very difficult to form new relationships and friendships
because of that uneasiness within me.
What about your health?
How has your health been affected?
I had lost a lot of weight.
I didn't feel very nourished.
When I ate, I felt like I was never full.
My health definitely needed to improve.
In the midst of all this, what were the kinds of dreams you were having?
I had been having certain nightmares at night.
Sometimes, I was hanging in my dreams
or I would dream of the ocean or a big storm or I was falling or things like that.
You mentioned as well that this led to suicidal thoughts.
Just explain a little more, how severe were these?
I would get impulsions if I saw any sharp object of any kind
and would imagine sticking it into myself or in someone closest to me
and also of a gun to my head.
Thoughts like that would always fill my mind,
like jumping in front of a train or things like that.
I would never do it but it was always a torment.
So we really understand how so many years you have been tormented
by this terrible demonic attack.
We give glory to God that you came here to the Anointing Water Revival in UK and the Lord touched you.

When the Anointing Water was ministered,

I could not control my words.
It was like I had no control over anything I was doing.
It was very different.

I am feeling lighter; I feel very light, actually.
I feel like I have lost a sense of heaviness.
The Lord is working on my mind now and the Holy Spirit has delivered me.
