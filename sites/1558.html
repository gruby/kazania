GOOD MORNING & WIN TODAY
- TB Joshua
Dear brethren, after the event of yesterday, the 8th of September 2019,
I think it would be necessary for me to devote two weeks of this month
to share with you the message in my heart and to explain
why I couldn’t hold back the tears during that song ministration which has now gone viral.
For the first time, all of you saw me betraying that very human emotion.
I pray that after these two weeks, the Lord will allow me
to continue addressing you all via this medium of social media.
Let us talk. Where do we start from? As the Lord directs. Thank you.
First of all, let me thank you for your prayers and support in all that life brings.
The following are some of the most common questions
people ask me in emails, letters, text message and phone calls:
“We would like to invite you to a wedding. How do we invite you?
We would like to invite you to a funeral. How do we invite you?
We would like to invite you to an occasion. How do we invite you?”
Well, I would have loved to be present at all meetings, occasions, funerals and weddings
but each time I ask God about this,
the event would have been held before I would receive the answer.
What is the cause of the delay in answering?
The greater my strength, the faster the answer.
It’s not that I don’t get answers often; it’s just that there is so much demand.
Today, people don’t care whether God permits them or not.
That is why you see them attend every occasion without waiting for God’s answer.
This is why there is calamity and trouble.
Thank you for now. Stay blessed!
TT: Mel Flowers 12/09/2019
