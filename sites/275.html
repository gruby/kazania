I have a word of advice for the churches all over the world.
Healing, deliverance, prophecy and all of God's blessings are hindered by money.
It is not possible to heal people and collect money.
It is not possible because of corresponding power;
we cannot deliver and receive money.
That delivering gift is not for sale.
When it is time to heal, it is time to give what God has given us.
Today, you can see the church all over the world,
you can see that the age of healing, deliverance and prophecy seems to have passed.
It has not passed; Jesus is the same yesterday, today and forever.
It is money -
those gifts are not for sale.
