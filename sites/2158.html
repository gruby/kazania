PRAYER FOR VIEWERS
Viewers, anywhere you are right now, pray along with Prophet T.B. Joshua.
Use your faith to put a demand on the anointing.
Distance is not a barrier!
Whatever challenge you may be facing, touch your screen and receive, in Jesus' name!
Let us pray for the viewers.
This is the period of resurrection.
We know we are guilty of many things.
We believe Jesus died on the Cross for us.
This is a special grace.
He died on the Cross for us.
Every soul that cries, that demands for justice - He died on the Cross.
Victory! Victory! Hallelujah!
You that are crying - cry no more!
Whatever situation you are facing - here is justice!
Are you sick? Be healed, in the name of Jesus!
Be delivered, in the name of Jesus!
Be set free, in the name of Jesus!
Jesus! You died on the Cross for us and gave us victory!
Receive deliverance!
Receive your healing!
Whatever affliction in your bones, blood, tendons, wherever in your body -
receive your healing, in the name of Jesus!
Receive your deliverance, in the name of Jesus Christ!
Every reproach - be free!
Every injustice - be free, in the name of Jesus!
Be free, in the name of Jesus!
Be free, in the name of Jesus Christ!
Jesus knows your challenges.
Be free, in the name of Jesus!
Be free, in the name of Jesus Christ!
In Jesus Christ's name we pray!
Thank You, Jesus.
In Jesus' name.
