Are you in an attitude of prayer?
Like I have said:
Faith releases healing power.
Faith releases blessing.
Faith is like a vacuum that draws anointing from God.
And you are building faith.
You are like a ball that is not well pumped.
But the more you say, "Take more of me, give me more of You" you are pumping the ball.
You see the ball becoming big.
You are the ball.
So if you stop, you stop pumping and if you continue, the ball will continue to grow big.
The air that enters is that faith; the words you are saying, "Take more of me, give me more of You",
it is so strong - spirit prayer.
When you get that ball up to a standard, to the level you can play with it -
you begin to eat with Jesus, see visions, dream dreams.
Your heart will begin to talk to you.
You don't need to sleep to see a vision; it is our heart that talks to us.
You will be moving, your heart will be saying, "Don't go there, go back."
"Who is talking to me?" - it is your heart.
Pumped by faith - get it working.
See with your heart, not with your eyes.
Stretch your hand.
Every barrier between your heart and the Spirit of God, be broken, in Jesus' name!
We want to fight this barrier.
Whatever barrier between your heart and the Spirit of God, be removed, in the name of Jesus!
What could be the barrier?
It could be offence you are holding against your neighbour.
You say, "Over my dead body - I will not forgive."
Are you holding offence, grudges, hatred, bitterness or pain of the past?
Be removed, in the name of Jesus!
I want you to stretch your hand and begin to remove whatever satan might have used to connect you.
He uses pain of the past to connect us to himself.
He uses bitterness to connect us to himself.
He uses pain of the past to connect us to himself.
Whatever satan might have used to connect you to himself, begin to disconnect yourself!
