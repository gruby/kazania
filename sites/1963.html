Don't Quit - Dig Deep And Fight It Through
The enemies we feel when we have failed are discouragement, depression,
embarrassment, shame and hopelessness.
Don't quit. Dig deep and fight it through!
If you don't quit when you fail, you will ultimately succeed in life.
As a baby who is learning how to walk,
you start by hitting every piece of furniture in the house.
You fall and get up,
then fall again and get up again until you master your balance.
Life is like that.
We have to continue to keep getting up any time we fail.
Keep looking forward.
Don't waste your time lamenting your past record, past failure.
We often succeed through failure.
Failure is a test for our maturity.
We have a goal to reach that needs all of our energy.
True failure lies not in falling down.
You only truly fail when you stop getting back up and trying again.
These Bible passages will help you further.
Romans 8:35-39,
1 Corinthians 3:1-3,
Hebrews 12:1-3,
Galatians 4:1-7,
and Jeremiah 8:4.
Thank you.
