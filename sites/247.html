247 - The SECRETS Of CONTENTMENT TB Joshua

Hallelujah!
Jesus has seen you through already.
That is the meaning of your coming here today.
Hallelujah!
You may be seated.
Thank You, Lord.
That a man is rich does not mean he is better than you in the presence of God.
One of the richest in the nation or in the world
does not mean that in the presence of God, he has a better position than you.
Because of those things, he becomes your mentor.
When it comes to spiritual issues,
we don't look at wealth, possessions or fame.
We don't use these to choose the person to be your mentor.
The only thing the Bible tells us about this:
such a person must be a communicator between visible and invisible.
When you see a communicator,
one of the qualities you see in them is contentment.
They believe they are what God says they are
and they have what God says they have.
CONTENTMENT
They don't look at others, they don't compare to others, they are always content.
Look at the word 'contentment'.
To be content is to be satisfied, no matter how little or small...
How many of us are content here?
That is the challenge the world at large has today.
That is the challenge you are having.
More...
And the Lord knows that no matter what He gives you, you will continue to ask for more and more...
Because you are not content - that has affected your peace.
The peace of heart is not there.
You cannot have peace; it is not possible when you are not content.
You continue to run and run until you discover whom you are, "Oh no. God's time is the best".
Only those who are content know God's time for their lives.
Look at what you are involved in.
Your income cannot even pay your house rent.
Your house rent is more than your income.
How do you pay your house rent?
The little you have now - if you can be content, it will grow.
The moment you are content, you are leaving the battle for God.
Whether the prayer is answered or not...
You ask for bread and there is no bread - the Lord will still remain your Provider, your God.
You will not run from one church to spiritualist or witch doctor
because you believe your prayer is not answered because you are not content.
It is a gift of God.
It is a thing of the heart; contentment comes from the heart.
The first place we are to prosper is in our spiritual life.
The challenges we are having now:
Your financial and emotional life are so much bigger than your spiritual life.
When your spiritual life is 3, every other area in your life is 20, 30, 40.
CONTENTMENT
When you are content, no more fear, murmuring or complaining.
The opinion of people about you does not matter but the opinion of God.
No one knows you better than yourself.
Let them say whatever they want to say about you.
The SECRETS Of CONTENTMENT | TB Joshua
When you are not content, you listen to what people say about you.
What people say about you dictates the direction.
Your situation dictates the direction of your prayer.
Then, when are you going to say, "Thank You, Jesus"?
In this world, you cannot be zero-free.
When you are content, you'll wait for God's time.
When you are content, God's time will be your roadmap.
When you want to move, you say, "God's time".
When it is not God's time, when everybody is moving, you will just wait.
And when you move at God's time, everything looks beautiful.
The SECRETS Of CONTENTMENT | TB Joshua
Where God guides, He protects.
Let us wait for God's time.
Look at our lives, even our marriages...
When you talk of marriage, there are two things we must take note of:
the right time and the right person.
It is the right time that brings about the right person,
not right person brings about the right time.
If those two are mistaken, there will be trouble in marriage.
Tell your neighbour, "The right time and the right person."
The right time brings about the right person.
The SECRETS Of CONTENTMENT | TB Joshua
