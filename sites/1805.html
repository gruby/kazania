EMMANUEL TV OUTREACH IN PAKISTAN DURING LOCKDOWN
Emmanuel! We are here in Shield of Faith Church, Lahore, Pakistan
on behalf of the man of God, T.B. Joshua and the Emmanuel TV Partners.
We thank God for His love and grace.
In the lockdown situation around the world, people are inside their homes
and mostly have no food to eat, and are living from hand to mouth.
During this difficult time, the man of God, T.B. Joshua and the Emmanuel TV Partners
sent help for the needy - some food items:
flour, sugar, salt, red chilli, grains, beans, lentils, tea, milk, pickles, spices and rice.
Because of the virus, there is need for social distancing within the Emmanuel TVTeam.
The Emmanuel TV Team will pack bags to take to the needy's doorsteps.
We are thankful to the man of God, T.B. Joshua and the Emmanuel TV Partners for their love.
From Emmanuel School and Shield of Faith Church, Lahore, Pakistan, thank you.
EMMANUEL TV PAKISTAN OUTREACH TO THE NEEDY DURING LOCKDOWN
Emmanuel!
Praise the Lord!
Greetings in Jesus Christ's name.
As you know, all over the world, there is virus
and Pakistan is also affected by this virus.
Because of the virus, we were staying at our home for 14 or 15 days
and we were not able to buy food.
But we are very thankful for Emmanuel TV and the Emmanuel TV Partners
who sent us these things to Pakistan.
They sent us flour, lentils, grains, cooking oil, sugar, salt, rice, pickles and many other things.
From the bottom of my heart, I am thankful to the Emmanuel TV Partners
who stand with us in this difficult time and saved us from death.
If we didn't have this food, maybe we would die.
But thank God for the Emmanuel TV Partners who sent us this stuff -
the food and materials that we can live on.
