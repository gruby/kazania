SPECIAL MESSAGE FROM TB JOSHUA!
Viewers all over the world, quite a long time...
Well, God's time is the best.
Now is God's time.
It is God's time to commune with you.
I love you; Jesus loves you the more.
What can separate us from the love of Christ?
Nothing.
Absolutely nothing!
Merry Christmas and a Happy New Year!
Having shared Yuletide greetings with you,
let us go to the message of today - 'Offence'.
I will take my reading from the Book of Luke 17.
You know, everything big starts little.
Luke 17:1
"Jesus said to his disciples: 'Things that cause people to stumble
are bound to come, but woe to anyone through whom they come'."
Things that cause people to stumble are bound to come -
that is, bound to happen - occur everywhere.
Offence has become so rampant and epidemic
and it has eaten into the fibre of our everyday relationships - unavoidable.
It is impossible to live this life and not have the opportunity to be offended.
I mean, it is impossible that no offences should come.
It is not a question of opportunity to be offended but what your response will be.
For preparation for the New Year, we must let go of offence
because offence is the effective instrument used by satan
to blind us on our present condition.
I want to pray with you for the preparation of the New Year.
By the authority in the name of Jesus, I release you from all hurts!
I release you from deep hurts!
I release you from whatever caused by offence -
bitterness, jealousy, anger, pain in the past -
be released, in the name of Jesus Christ!
Be released, in the name of Jesus Christ!
I command that spirit, every spirit offence might have caused in your life -
jealousy, hatred, anger, inferiority, resentment, strifes, outrage.
I release you, in the name of Jesus!
Be released, in the name of Jesus!
Be released, in the name of Jesus!
In Jesus Christ's name I pray. Amen.
What is offence?
Offence is the use of negative words that register in the heart of the offended;
that brings about offence.
Honestly, offence is not deadly.
Feeding on it in our heart makes it deadly.
When we concentrate and focus on the wrong done to us,
we automatically forget the condition in which we are.
Focusing on ourselves is the effective instrument used by satan
to blind us on our present condition.
We say, "Why me? Why me of all this? Why me of all this accusation?
Why me of all this disappointment? Why me?"
While focusing on this, we automatically forget the reality of our condition -
no food on the table, no money in the pocket,
health challenges and so on and so forth.
In order to have Jesus Christ as our Lord and Saviour in this New Year,
we must let go of offence.
Right now, let us pray.
Whatever caused by offence in your life -
the spirit of jealousy, hatred, bitterness, anger -
I release you, in the name of Jesus Christ!
By the authority in the name of Jesus, begin to release yourself!
By the authority in the name of Jesus Christ,
I command that spirit in your heart - be free, in the name of Jesus!
Be free, in the name of Jesus!
You need Jesus in the New Year.
In order to have Jesus, you must let go of offence.
As I said, offence is very common due to lack of real love.
We have almost come to believe that it is a way of life.
The truth remains - our lives are centred on how we are doing,
feeling and how we are treated.
How we are doing, feeling and treated is not about us but Jesus.
If the Word of God is true for your life, say after me:
'I believe You have answered my prayers.
I will continue to live under Your protection
and dwell in Your shadow the rest of my life. Amen'.
Viewers, thank you for your prayers and support.
We shall meet on Emmanuel TV live on the New Year's Eve Service.
Thank you very much.
Once again, in order to have Jesus as your Lord and Saviour this New Year,
you must let go of offence.
You know what offence has caused us -
bitterness, anger, strifes, outrage, resentment, jealousy, envy
and so on and so forth.
When you retain an offence in your heart, you filter everything through it.
You laugh, pray, smile and preach through it.
You sleep through it - this is the cause of nightmares.
Once again, thank you.
I can't wait to see you live on Emmanuel TV
for the New Year's Eve Service where we shall have the opportunity
to share with you God's opinion about you, your nation and your continent.
Thank you. God bless. Jesus is Lord.
Emmanuel!
(God with us - Matthew 1:23)
