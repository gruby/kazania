(God With Us - Matthew 1:23)
My name is Moses Eremi.
I am from Bayelsa State.
I want to thank God for the great deliverance that God did for me last Monday.
I was born in Nembe, Bayelsa State.
Nembe is a kingdom and located there is Ogidiga - the name of the god of that land.
Everybody gives respect to the idol of that land.
Nobody can trespass the laws attached to that deity.
That is how it has been.
After some time, I gave my life to Christ when I was about 16 years old.
Then I began to have some experiences in my dreams - I had sex with people I didn't know.
Sometimes, I would experience suffocation.
When I would wake up, I would see someone pressing me and so on.
I was suffering, crying and trying to find out what the problem was.
I never knew that there was anything called deliverance.
I preached the Gospel with zeal.
People knew me for that.
Every morning, I would go out for the morning call or evangelism and follow-up.
That was who I was.
Then, I discovered in 1996 when I was praying about what I was seeing in my dreams,
one day, something happened in the house.
You say you preached the Gospel, you evangelise.
Yes, sir.
- You preach to people.
- Yes, sir.
- About Jesus.
- Yes, sir.
Listen to that.
So, how you yourself know that you are delivered?
Ask your neighbour, "How do you know you are delivered?"
Do you listen to that?
She is an Evangelist, she preaches Gospel, she goes from city to city to preach the Gospel.
Ask your neighbour again, "How do you know you are delivered?"
Because you preach the Gospel?
Ask your neighbour, "Because you preach the Gospel?"
Are you sure it is a pure one?
In 1996 while I was praying, something visited me in the house.
It was as if the thing fell from my roof and split the ground open.
The thing came under the bed and then pressed against my mouth.
I tried to shout, 'Jesus' but I couldn't.
After that, I started to experience a giant man who would come to me.
Even when I was in the midst of people, I used to sense when the thing was coming.
Just like that - after some years...
- While you were preaching the Gospel?
- Yes, sir.
Listen to that.
That does not stop you from preaching, as long as you don't preach the pure one.
I suffered under the influence of these demons.
I would fall and rise.
In my church, everybody saw me as someone who was not stable.
It made me cry and that's when I started searching for a solution.
I used to experience that anytime I went to my village,
my prayer life would cease and I would struggle.
In the year 2000, I woke up one morning and started to cry on the 1st January
and I asked God why I couldn't pray when I wanted to pray.
That day, after meditation, I slept and saw a huge giant around my area.
He was very dark and as tall as the Iroko tree.
His eyes, mouth and palms were red and he was naked.
When I sighted him, I began to run.
He turned into the wind, carried me and hit me against the wall and said, "Come and be one of my leaves.
You are running away. Every Nembe person belongs to this tree.
I will make you bow before me. You will continue to fall.
I will make you fall until you come and bow before me."
I couldn't call the name, Jesus.
But I said in my mind that even if Jesus did not save me, I would serve Him till I die.
Then, the wind left me and I woke up.
After that, I started having strange experiences, whereby I had abominable relationships.
In 2001, I told my mother about the dream I had.
I described the place where I saw the thing.
She said that place was called Sotugum.
All the experiences and abominable relationships I went into
were to bring me to bow physically before him.
When my Christian life became so unstable, I began to worry.
I started to looking for a solution until 2009 when I saw the man of God on Emmanuel TV.
When one of my relationships was on the verge of breaking,
I began to look for a solution and I saw the man of God preaching.
I understood his preaching and became convinced that I could get my deliverance here.
I came and said that I would spend my holiday here and that God would visit me before I went back.
So, I have been here for three weeks.
When the Wise Men came to pray, I said, "God, the anointing in this place cannot escape me.
I must receive deliverance here.
Any darkness that is in me, I must not go back with it; I must drop it here. This is the arena of liberty."
I got angry within myself and I prayed that prayer.
When the man of God started praying, I started to feel uncomfortable.
So, I handed my phone to my son.
The next moment, I was 'off'.
I thank God today that I am delivered, completely separated from whatever
or whoever was tormenting me.
Hallelujah.
We thank God for your life.
That's wonderful.
TT Robert Begier 21/07/2020
