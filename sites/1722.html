TESTIMONY CONTINUES
My name is Mrs Marian Eze.
Next to me is my husband.
We are from Imo State, Nigeria.
I am here to testify to the goodness of the Lord in my life.
One morning on February, I went to pray with a group of people.
The person was lying on the floor.
After praying we all left and I went back to my house.
On getting to my house, I went to my chair to sit.
Immediately I sat on that chair, something like thunder hit me on my back.
It ran fast like a snake and tied my neck.
It was so bad.
I was trying to stand up to make some noise but I passed out.
I don't know what I was doing, I don't even know how everything was going on in my life
since that February till last week, when my husband said we should come to The SCOAN.
From the moment we entered a bus till arriving here, I was not aware of anything.
My body was weak, my legs, my eyes, everything was just off.
They put me on the prayer line, I was prayed for by the man of God.
Immediately I was prayed for, I got instant healing.
Since February till last week, I didn't do anything.
He was the one taking care of the children, washing, cooking - everything was done by him.
Immediately that touch was on me, my healing came instantly.
