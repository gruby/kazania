Viewers all over the world, where can we go from His presence?
Jesus knows your situation.
He knows your disposition.
He knows your heart's desire.
All you need now - heart.
Wherever you are, what matters - your heart.
Begin to receive, in the name of Jesus!
Be free, in the name of Jesus!
I command all spirits operating in your life to leave you, in the name of Jesus!
Spirit of failure, nightmare, stagnation, setback - move, in the name of Jesus!
Leave my people, in the name of Jesus!
You devil, remove your hands from my people!
Your hands of failure - move, in the name of Jesus!
Leave my people, in the name of Jesus!
Your hands of disappointment, your hands of failure, your hands of affliction -
leave, in the name of Jesus!
I know you are delivered!
I know you are healed!
I know you are delivered!
I know you are free!
In Jesus' name!
Thank You, Lord.
TT: Mel Flowers 27/08/2019
