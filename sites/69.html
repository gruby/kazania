Anywhere you are, distance is not a barrier.
Viewers, you are called to battle the spiritual entities
which cause crisis in your lives.
You and Prophet T.B. Joshua together, in the name of Jesus,
can stop satan killing, stealing and destroying your life.
Touch the screen, right now.
ENGAGE WITH GOD
Viewers all over the world,
where can you go from His presence?
Whatever situation you are in,
Jesus loves you.
Right now, give thanks to Him for His love.
Ask Him to come in His love and bless you,
and save you
and deliver you.
Come in Your love, oh Lord, and bless me.
Come in Your love, oh Lord, and heal me.
Come in Your love, oh Lord, and free me.
I command the love of God in your life, in your soul, in your spirit,
in the name of Jesus!
Begin to enjoy the love of God in your health,
love of God in your finances,
love of God in your ministry,
love of God in your nation.
love of God in your career.
love of God in your destiny.
Come in Your love and restore.
Come in Your love and deliver.
Come in Your love and save us.
Come in your love and bless us.
In Jesus' name!
Pray Along With Prophet T.B. Joshua
Touch Your Screen By Faith In Jesus' Name
We leave you under the influence of the Holy Spirit
and pray to meet you there again.
Allow your heart to be deeply engaged with God
as you send your testimony.
ENGAGE WITH GOD
