I join this family. My name should be T.B. Joshua Onyeka.
The prophecy is 1,000 percent true.
There is a big problem in my family.
I went to South Africa in 1997, which is 15 years ago now
but any time I planned to come to Nigeria there would be problems;
serious problems whereby I would lose everything I had.
Even my wife wanted to come with me to Nigeria to establish herself
but died in a car accident when she planned to come on March 17th, 2008
and she left this boy with me.
Things were very difficult. I could not even afford to feed the boy, he stayed with his cousins.
Even at this stage to pay my house rent is a problem.
I had tried to come to Nigeria but each time I tried there would be a big problem.
This time I had to mortgage my car to come here because
I started having attacks and seeing dead people in my dreams.
I mortgaged my car to come to Nigeria
because I have tried everything and I believed that The SCOAN would be my last bus stop.
Even as I was coming from South Africa I wrote it down that I said to God,
'Oh God, I am not commanding You but The SCOAN is my last bus stop.
If You don't answer my prayer at The SCOAN, I think it is better I go to the grave.'
You mean you wrote this statement in the airplane when you were coming, saying,
'Oh God, I am not commanding You but The SCOAN is my last bus stop.
If nothing is achieved here, I still believe that You are God,
but I think it would be better for me to go down to the grave.'
You mean you came to The SCOAN believing that it was your last bus stop,
and if God Almighty didn't answer your situation, you were going to kill yourself. Is that what that statement means?
Exactly because there was no point of my living.
For 15 years I never saw my mother or my family, if not for my younger brother.
We thank God Almighty that He intervened in your situation.
During the prophecy the man of God also said that there was a sudden death.

The first accident I had, you can see here
and here.
This was from the first accident and in the second accident my friend who was driving died.
The man of God also spoke about 'sudden death'. What can you say about that?
There is sudden death in my life because even my wife died in a car accident.
Ever since that time, I kept seeing dead people in my dreams and having attacks.
On 5th of January I received a phone call while I was sleeping.
It was like a dream. When I received that phone call, I said, 'Hello. Who is that?' -
my head just swallowed and I shouted.
I believe that there was a demon that entered that room
and I wanted to remove my clothes and run away from there.
My father died in 2001 with sudden death while I was in South Africa.
Since he died, I never came to Nigeria because anytime I tried to come to Nigeria there would be a big problem
and I would lose everything I had.
I had never come to Nigeria since then, this is my first time coming back to Nigeria.
And the man of God said you were here for a purpose.
Can you tell us why you are here and can you confirm the prophecy of the man of God, Prophet T.B. Joshua?
What he said is true because I came with a purpose.
Since my wife died there was an accident fund that the lawyer was supposed to pay for me to establish myself,
but since then, they never paid me anything and I had been
trying everything possible to come to Nigeria to see my mother and brothers.
I came here for deliverance because I knew that what was happening to me was not natural but a spiritual attack,
which I could not save myself from.
I believe that only God could save me through Prophet T.B. Joshua
because I was always seeing how God was using Him to save many people.
That was why I said that if I come to The SCOAN and my prayers were not answered,
it would be better for me to go to the grave because I didn't have any other hope.
