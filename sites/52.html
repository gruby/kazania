52


Viewers all over the world, at God's time healing happens,
deliverance happens and salvation takes place.
It is God's time for your miracle!
Allow God's Word to live in you,
to get into your blood
and grow to gain supremacy over you
as you prepare your hearts to join Prophet T.B. Joshua in prayer
to bring about the kind of conversion
that comes from the Living Word
and receive the breakthrough
that will make you forget the pain of the past,
in Jesus' name.
GET READY FOR REVIVAL!!!
T.B. Joshua Viewers Prayer
Viewers all over the world, listen to me!
Whatever medium you are using to view us,
I say to you, in the name of Jesus,
whatever situation you are in, trust Jesus.
Trust Jesus.
Trust Jesus for your healing.
Right now, disease, sickness, affliction in your life -
I command them out, in the name of Jesus!
I command them out of your system!
Say: Out of my system!
You affliction,
you infirmity,
disease,
sickness in my fluid,
in my tendons,
in my blood,
out, in the name of Jesus!
Out!
GET READY FOR REVIVAL!!!
T.B. Joshua Viewers Prayer
Viewers all over the world, get ready for revival in your business.
Viewers all over the world, get ready for revival in your career,
in your finances, in your ministry.
Right now, in the name of Jesus, revival!
Revival, in the name of Jesus!
In your business, revival!
In your finances, revival!
In your ministry, revival!
Revival, in your career!
Revival!
Thank You, Lord.
TT Rae van Zyl 05/02/2021
