TESTIMONY CONTINUES
My name is Mrs Chinwe Eheli.
I am a Nigerian residing in South Africa.
Last week Sunday I came straight from the airport to The SCOAN.
Arriving to the church, I was located by the man of God.
The first prophecy that came was mine.
I and some other people came out.
The man of God looked at me and said, "You are the one I am referring to.
It is not guesswork."
He said, "It is not guesswork" twice.
I am here standing to tell you that when the Holy Spirit located me, it was not guesswork indeed.
I have been having this problem for about two and a half years now.
I had to deal with this bleeding.
You expect yourself to finish your menstrual cycle in 4 days.
When it gets to 4 weeks, 6 weeks, there is something else.
As a result of that I started having iron deficiency.
This iron deficiency was so strong that I had to use 'iron tablets' all the time.
I would drink 'iron tablets', I would use medications for sportsmen
just to elevate myself, to get a little bit of energy.
It went on and on like that.
It was not only the bleeding - the bleeding led to iron deficiency.
That iron deficiency graduated to eating soil.
I would go to the market, buy any soil there.
Sometimes, if I ran out of soil, I could go out, clean the top of any soil and eat it.
Now, the soil caused me to have constipation - I was not able to use the toilet as I should.
The piles came as a result of constipation.
This bleeding I am talking about - it is not just bleeding.
I would be bleeding from the normal menstrual cycle and from the anus,
so, it was double bleeding.
Because already a part of the anus was coming out, I had a wound there
that bled all the time, whenever I went to the toilet.
It was a hassle indeed.
It affected me so much, because as a woman I was always very tired.
I could not perform my duties as a wife.
I couldn't cook food because when I was tired I could not do anything.
My husband would be assisting me here and there.
Also, I could not perform my duties as a woman in the bedroom.
You understand what I mean.
It was to the extent that my husband ended up asking me,
"My wife, when I call you in the morning, you are not there.
I call you in the afternoon, you say at night. When it is night, you are not there."
And I am like, "You know what? Let's give God time."
About two weeks before coming here, he even said to me,
"Don't tell me you are going to Nigeria just like that."
And I was like, "The thing is still there. I don't know when it is going to stop."
At least, I could perform my duties as a wife.
I realised that this was not ordinary.
I was motivated because my husband came here last year and man of God gave him a prophecy.
He said that there was a man who had a prostate cancer.
When he came back, he was fine, so I was highly motivated by that.
I told myself, "I am going to The SCOAN."
That was the main problem that brought me to The SCOAN.
When I finally came to The SCOAN, the man of God said,
"There is a woman who is bleeding and she also has piles."
I could feel deep in my spirit that I was the one the man of God was talking to.
I came out and the man of God confirmed it, by saying, "This is the woman I am referring to.
It is not guesswork."
After the service I was taken to the restroom.
Instantly...
I couldn't believe what happened, I thought it was a joke.
I wiped and there was nothing.
The bleeding stopped instantly.
That is the evidence of Jesus Christ - lives changed.
Since last Sunday, I was still using a pad, thinking it would still come out somehow.
Because this thing - what it did to me in the aircraft...
I had a packet of pads, with 8 of them inside.
I thought it was going to be enough for the trip.
I found out that it was like a tap was open and it kept flowing.
It even stained the chair of the aircraft.
I think I went to the toilet more than 6 times, as if I was working in the toilet.
I didn't know what to do.
As a woman, I had to try by all means to put myself together.
In Nigeria they call it 'packaging'.
I packaged myself well, just that people would not see.
When I packaged myself, I came out as if nothing had happened.
Then I would sit and then I heard... and I jumped out of my chair and ran back to the toilet.
That trip was not enjoyed because of this problem.
Today, people of God, I am here to tell you that the problem is over
and I thank God so much for the deliverance.
I am back to normal.
I am no more bleeding.
I felt the pigmentation of blood in my palms.
That was the first thing that happened to me,
that confirmed to me that indeed I have been delivered.
My word of advice will be:
People of God - put God first.
I am a Nigerian; I haven't seen my mother for 4 years,
but I chose to come here straight because I know what God means to me.
So, I came from the airport straight, believing that God will see me through
and indeed that happened.
So, if you want a divine turnaround, learn to put God first in all you do.
Can you tell us how are you now, concerning that piles?
People of God, I have to confirm this:
This piles sometimes made not to be able to sit straight.
I would be sitting one-sided.
I would even cross my legs, as if it was a style.
There was no style there.
I just sat one-sided just to make myself comfortable because it didn't allow me to sit straight.
From that day henceforth, I am healed.
No more piles, no more pains.
I can sit nicely like a lady.
Before, this is how I sat.
I would go this way and I would cross my legs
because I would avoid sitting with my back straight,
because the pain came straight from the inside.
But now I can sit nicely, as you can see.
I can even... - just for you see - nicely.
Hallelujah!
Thank You, Jesus.
Let's put our hands together for Jesus Christ.
Thank You, Jesus.
When you have health challenges...
Most of us would agree with me that most of our prayer points would be "Breakthrough",
until you have health challenges.
You will not remember that "Breakthrough", you will push it aside,
and all you would be hearing, "Healing",
because only a person that is alive would look for money.
Praise God!
TT Robert Begier 23/08/2020
RV Gary Tonge 24/08/2020
