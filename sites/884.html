Prophet T.B. Joshua's Arrival
In The Nation Of Paraguay
One of the top prestigious newspapers in Paraguay, on Thursday, the 10th of August 2017
featured Prophet T.B. Joshua in their article titled,
"Nigerian Preacher, TB Joshua, Awarded Paraguay's Highest Honour – The National Order Of Merit".
The national Parliament of Paraguay has concurred to grant the "Comuneros" National Order of Merit
to Nigerian Prophet TB Joshua.
This was decided August 9th 2017, at the sitting of the parliament in Asuncion,
according to 970 AM Radio journalist, Nativida Romero.
Member of Parliament Bernardo Villalba (ANR) proposed
that the spiritual leader, Temitope Balogun Joshua, receive the highest distinction
the Parliament can bestow.
The legislator's proposal was fully approved.
Avanze Pais MP, Rocio Casco, did however oppose it on the ground that the distinction had previously
only been awarded to citizens of the country.
For his part, the legislator Villalba maintained his position.
Villalba put forward his arguments, in full session, where he expressed that Joshua,
compelled to visit Paraguay, "will bring peace and blessing to the population".
"I do not just want to welcome him to our country.
We pray that his stay be as fruitful as possible
and that it will be a blessing to our nation.
For the first time ever, I propose the National Order to be awarded to a personality and I am proposing that
he be awarded this distinction by the Members of Parliament," said Villalba.
Villalba solicited that the preacher be presented the national honour at the Defensores del Chaco Stadium,
where he will host the crusade.
