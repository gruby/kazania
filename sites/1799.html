Good morning, my name is Usiwoma. I am a Doctor in Timișoara, Romania.
I have here with me Dr. Oana.
Hello!
When the coronavirus started, especially when we started having cases in Romania and in my city,
it really caused panic because we usually attend to patients from all over the country
and even outside the country.
I remember one of my colleagues saying that she was just waiting to die.
How did you feel when you heard about the coronavirus?
I felt a bit worried, but I was more concerned for my parents because they are older.
And you?
I kept on praying with Emmanuel TV and it really strengthened me.
On Sunday, the prophet posted an announcement on Facebook
that there will be showers of rain that will wipe away the fear of the unknown.
I noticed also that it started raining, so I immediately ran outside and made sure I got wet.
Each day, I prayed with the prophet for the patients that had coronavirus and for the doctors.
Viewers all over the world, no one enjoys being sick.
Lord Jesus, Your children need Your healing hand - heal them, in the name of Jesus!
The right hand of God is power.
Whatever virus, wherever it is in the body - heal them, in the name of Jesus!
Those on the sickbed,
may your doctors, nurses, caregivers sense the presence of God, in the name of Jesus!
Sense the presence of God, in the name of Jesus!
On the 23rd, there was some news on national TV called 'Știrile PRO TV'
where it reported that 51 out of 54 patients were healed in a hospital in Timișoara;
how did the doctors do it?
And there was a video of the Head of the Section of Infectious Diseases
and under it, it said, "A hope of healing in the fight against this fearful virus comes from Timișoara."
I got calls from all over the world, from abroad.
Right now, we have received a phone call from Switzerland asking us to tell them our protocol.
It has been heard everywhere that we have very very good results.
At your hospital in Timișoara, you have the highest number of healed patients. What is the secret?
The secret of the cured people from Timișoara is the favourable conjuncture and seriousness
of the team of doctors and nurses who participate in the treatment of these patients.
In total, in Timișoara, out of 54 patients found positive with coronavirus, 51 were already discharged.
Thus the Hospital for Infectious Diseases in Timișoara became the centre of Romania,
with the highest rate of patients cured of COVID-19.
I was really grateful to God because I know He helped the doctors choose the right treatment protocol.
As was reported, all the patients recovered between 3 to 5 days.
How it is done is that they are tested, and after being negative,
they have to wait for 14 days and get tested again and if they are still negative,
they are discharged to their home.
When I made a calculation of this time, it all pointed to after the rain;
that is when God really started doing this work.
There was lots of rejoicing, calmness, and donations to the hospital.
How did you feel after you heard all the news?
I was happy because I know that God turns every bad situation into something good.
As the Bible says in Jeremiah,
" 'For I know the plans and thoughts I have for you,' says the Lord,
'plans for peace and well-being and not for disaster, to give you a future and a hope.' "
And you?
I was really grateful to God, encouraged and strengthened.
After some days, we got an announcement in the hospital that we will start operating
on patients with the coronavirus who have surgical emergencies, if any cases arise.
I kept on praying and the following Sunday, as the prophet always does,
he prays for patients with coronavirus and he prays for doctors,
but on that specific Sunday, after the announcement in the hospital,
he prayed for surgeons and I was amazed!
Those on the sickbed - Lord Jesus, guide the medical practitioners, in the name of Jesus Christ!
Guide the surgeon's hands.
May their bodies respond quickly to treatment, in the name of Jesus Christ!
Father, thank You, Lord.
In Jesus Christ's name. Amen.
It really strengthened me and encouraged me, knowing that God is concerned about patients with coronavirus.
He is concerned about the doctors, nurses and everyone involved in taking care of these patients.
There is something the prophet always says, that we should not fear.
Medically, when there is constant fear, it causes the cortisol in our bodies
to reduce the immune-boosting and the infection-fighting cells.
This means our immune system is going to be weakened against fighting infection,
or even cancer.
We really need our immune system at this time.
Also, when there is fear and anxiety, it reduces the amount of oxygen
and glucose going to our heart and large muscles.
It can also lead to heart problems.
It can also affect our reasoning, our brain.
It can cause ulcers, it leads to early ageing and even premature death.
So, as much as we wash our hands, keep social distancing and boost our immune system,
it is very important that we protect our immune system by refusing to fear.
Right now, begin to plead the Blood of Jesus against every fear!
Fear of cancer, virus, coronavirus, affliction, disappointment, fear of this and that -
begin to plead the Blood of Jesus against every fear!
Resist fear, in the name of Jesus!
Begin to resist fear, in the name of Jesus!
Let us relax in Jesus, the Name above coronavirus, knowing that God is faithful and true.
Emmanuel!
