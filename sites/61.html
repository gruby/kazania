Viewers all over the world, where can you go from His presence?
Jesus is saying: I see you; I know you.
I know your disposition,
I know your thoughts,
I know where you are.
Get ready, right now.
Every demonic attack,
every spirit that stands against you,
be broken, in the name of Jesus!
Every spirit hindering your blessing,
every spirit hindering your finances,
every spirit hindering your business,
every spirit hindering your career,
every spirit hindering your marriage,
every spirit tormenting your life,
in the name of Jesus, be broken!
Whatever situation you are in, never a sickness Jesus cannot heal.
Whatever sickness,
whatever situation,
whatever disease,
whatever bondage -
never a burden Jesus cannot bear,
never a sickness Jesus cannot heal,
never a problem Jesus cannot solve.
Be broken!
Be set free!
I can see you being set free.
I can see you being saved.
I can see you being healed.
I can see you being blessed.
In the name of Jesus!
JESUS IS SAYING, "I SEE YOU, I KNOW YOU"
