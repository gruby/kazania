over your business, in the name of Jesus Christ,
over your health, in the name of Jesus Christ!

never a sickness Jesus cannot heal, never a disease Jesus cannot cure.

Break it, in the name of Jesus!

be broken, in the name of Jesus Christ!
I can see your business being released, in the name of Jesus Christ!

Your life, your destiny, career are being released, in the name of Jesus Christ!
Thank You, Jesus Christ! In Jesus Christ's name.
