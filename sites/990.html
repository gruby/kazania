C.S. Upthegrove fought this good fight.
He fought it out and finished his course.
He did not give over till he was made more than a conqueror through God who loved him.
Evangelist C.S Upthegrove ranked among God's generals, ministered in the healing power of God
and preached the hope of the gospel of Jesus Christ to many in the US and around the world.
He began preaching the Word of God at the age of 15, and began seeing signs and wonders
in his revival meetings, where the sick were healed, the demon possessed were delivered
and the lost were saved.
In 1965, as a disciple of God's general A.A. Allen, he was ordained pastor of Miracle Valley in Arizona USA,
and people began coming from far and wide to receive their salvation, healing and deliverance.
This ministry continued until evangelist C.S. Upthegrove's peaceful passing.
In the late hours of Sunday, January 28th, 2018, the crown of life was placed upon his head.
Family and loved ones surrounded him, grateful to have had him in their midst.
Death to a good man is his release from the imprisonment of this world and his departure
to the enjoyments of another world.
He does not cease to be but is only removed from one world to another.
His legacy continues.
The best prayer we can offer our friends, family and loved ones is that the Lord Jesus Christ
may be with their spirits, to satisfy and save them and at the last, to receive them to Himself.
Many who believed as the God's generals did, are now before the throne, giving glory to the Lord.
May we be followers of them.
