1976


Prophet T.B. Joshua Ministering Healing And Deliverance In Jesus' Name
Prophet T.B. Joshua is praying for the nursing mothers in the prayer line at The SCOAN.
He prays for Mrs Tina Itimi who is booked for an operation and has come for safe delivery.
In the mighty name of Jesus Christ!
As Prophet T.B. Joshua prays for her,
the healing power of our Lord Jesus Christ is being released into her system
and immediately you can see the baby beginning to move under the influence of the Holy Spirit.
Thank You, Lord.
The man of God moves on to pray for others
that have come to the prayer line with various problems and afflictions.
They are believing that God will take them out of their problems
and they shall not go back, in Jesus' name.
Prophet T.B. Joshua returns to pray for Mrs Itimi.
Tears roll down her face during the prayer indicating that the power of God is present.
Begin to share your testimony. Thank you.
Amen.
As Prophet T.B. Joshua declares them all free in the name of Jesus Christ,
Mrs Itimi slowly stands and seems to be in a state of discomfort.
God's anointed servant, T.B. Joshua, rushes to a private room where Mrs Itimi has been taken for delivery.
Signs of labour are apparent.
She is experiencing contractions and is breathing heavily.
She lies on the floor as T.B. Joshua offers Spirit prayer over her condition.
Thank You, Lord.
In no time at all the baby is born!
Hallelujah!
Glory be to God for He makes impossibilities possible!
There is no need greater than our God.
This was done so that the generations to come may believe in Him and trust in His goodness.
TESTIMONY TIME
My name is Mrs Tina Itimi.
This man standing behind me is my husband,
and the woman beside me is my husband's sister.
My husband's sister brought me here.
It is my first time coming to The Synagogue Church Of All Nations.
The doctor at the hospital told me that I would have to deliver my baby through an operation
because I have a tight cervix, my baby is in the wrong position and is not breathing.
When Prophet T.B. Joshua prayed for me,
I began to feel some signs in my body
and I delivered my baby safely.
Mrs Tina Itimi - Baby Delivery Testimony
My name is Mr John Itimi.
The problem that brought us to The Synagogue Church Of All Nations
was the inability of my wife to give birth to our baby.
We were in the hospital that morning when she couldn't give birth
and the doctor said that she had a tight cervix
and the only way for her to give birth is through an operation.
I called my eldest sister on the telephone and said,
"Sister, this is the problem we have at the hospital.
We can't control the situation".
Immediately she said that she had to take us to The Synagogue Church Of All Nations.
She believed, that by the grace of God, when the anointed man of God came, everything would be over.
That is how we were rushed down to The Synagogue Church Of All Nations.
They arranged us in the prayer line.
When the man of God, Prophet T.B. Joshua, came out,
he lay an anointed hand on my wife
and immediately the Spirit of God fell on her
and that is how she gave birth without an operation. Glory be to God!
TT Rae van Zyl 27/08/2020
