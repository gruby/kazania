Dominican Republic Crusade With TB Joshua - Day 1
My name is Floranny.
I am 27 years old and I am from The Dominican Republic.
I had an issue on my spine.
I was not able to move my neck - I had to use a neck collar -
and I also had fibromyalgia.
The doctors told me that I had to use it for six months to see whether surgery was necessary.
It affected me because I was not able to lean back,
I was not able to turn my neck to the right or to the left or backwards.
I was not able to do the things that I was usually able to do before.
When Prophet TB Joshua prayed for me I started to feel how the Holy Spirit touched my soul
and touched my body, and I fell down.
After that when I came back up,
I started to feel that the right side of my body was not in pain anymore
and I was able to turn my neck to the right and to the left after that.
I was not able to do this.
I was not able to move my neck.
So I am good now.
I feel fine. The pain is gone!
Thank You, Jesus!
My advice is that whatever you are going through
or whatever issue you have -
it does not matter whether it is a medical pain, a physical pain
or something that is in your spirit, something that you feel -
I encourage you to follow Jesus and to look for His Word, search for His Word,
and trust that He is able to heal anything that you have.
Thank You, Jesus, for Your healing.
