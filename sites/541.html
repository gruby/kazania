My name is Hussein Mohammed from Edo State, Nigeria.
The problem that brought me to The SCOAN was that I possessed the demon of an old woman.

Whenever the woman came, the picture in my parlour would fall down
and when I went to the office, I would find that the same picture had fallen down there as well.
Anytime this woman came, things would be very difficult for me
I wouldn't be able to do anything.
I had been going to many places - churches, night vigils
whereas others fell down, I would remain standing.
