Mr Fabian Idika, a police officer from Ebonyi State Nigeria,
has come to The Synagogue Church Of All Nations with the problem of leg ulcer due to a gun shot,
and also uses a walking stick for support.
The condition of his leg is in a horrendous state -
dead skin covers the surface of the leg and a bullet buried inside for many years.
This is an extreme case that has defied all medical solution.
The problem has gone beyond the power of nature,
and that is why Mr Fabian Idika has decided to commit his life under Someone wiser,
more clever and more powerful than himself: Jesus Christ who raises the dead!
As the camera pans to the right,
we can see an open wound has formed on the inner part of his leg below the calf muscle.
Mr Fabian Idika is believing Jesus Christ to rescue him from his deplorable health condition.
