FLESH EATING ULCER DESTROYED
Barely able to walk due to a rotting leg covered with wounds,
this lady hobbles along, assisted by a man on one side and a walking stick on the other.
Her strife is a harsh reality.
Her pain is felt as she slowly moves with this shameful and painful condition.
The leg cancer has made this young lady old due to the torment it has caused her to go through;
pain, isolation and rejection because of the nature of the disease.
This is Paulina Godwin's lower leg.
Her anguish is obvious due to the raw and fragile state of the cancerous wounds.
Her skin is peeling, as the cancer destroys from the inside out.
The 28 year old's leg is so ravaged it is difficult to behold,
but for the glory of God, she willingly shows the horrendous state
the deadly disease has caused her lower leg.
My name is Mrs Paulina Godwin.
I am 28 years old.
I woke up and discovered that I had pain in my leg.
That night, when I touched that very place, it was very hot.
That was how my leg started to become deformed.
From then, it became like this.
Since this problem began, I have not been feeling well.
The pain - I cannot withstand it.
Since then, I have been feeling so much pain - in my head, heart and various other places.
I believe that I will receive my healing today.
Paulina's leg cancer has affected her greatly but it has not affected her faith.
Despite the immense pain, she still believes Jesus Christ is the same,
the Master Healer able to heal her.
This is the moment of prayer.
T.B. Joshua stands close by with his arm outstretched towards Mrs Godwin's leg.
With his shoe removed, T.B. Joshua uses his own leg as a point of contact
for the Holy Spirit to use to bring healing to Paulina's decaying leg.
Holding her walking stick, he motions for her to stand, which she does unassisted.
She then proceeds to walk along without help.
This instant change is a sign that healing has taken place.
A few weeks later, Mrs Paulina Godwin came back with a different story to tell -
one of victory, restoration and God's grace.
Let's watch.
FLESH EATING ULCER DESTROYED
This time, Mrs Paulina Godwin proudly shows us her healed and healthy leg.
To God be the glory.
Only small marks remain as proof that she was once terribly sick
with a rotting leg, as seen on the other side of the screen.
There are no natural words to describe this, except to say, "Thank You, Jesus Christ",
who is worthy to be praised.
Madam, can you tap the place where you were having the pain before?
No pain.
- Could you tap it like this before?
- No.
Can you stamp the foot on the floor?
- Are you having pain?
- No pain.
- Could you do it like that before?
- Not at all.
- Why?
- Because of the pain.
- But right now?
- I am OK.
TESTIMONY TIME
My name is Paulina Godwin.
The first time I came, I was brought here by my husband and some of my friends.
The pain was so much that I could not even sleep at night.
I could not walk. I could not eat.
I could not even go to the toilet or use the bathroom by myself.
I was placed on the prayer line.
Prophet T.B. Joshua stretched his hand towards my leg
and stretched his own toes on my knees.
Instantly, I received my healing and stood up from that moment and started walking.
The man of God did not collect anything from me;
rather, he is the one giving me money.
- Are you feeling any pain?
- No pain at all.
One of the visitors listening to Paulina's testimony
steps forward to touch her leg, confirming Christ's perfect healing.
