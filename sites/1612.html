HEALED OF POOR VISION THROUGH EMMANUEL TV!!!
My name is Helena Mbanga from Tanzania.
I am here to give a testimony after praying along with Prophet T.B. Joshua on Emmanuel TV.
I could not read or write anything without using spectacles for over 20 years!
As a teacher, I had a difficult time in preparing notes and marking my books and exams.
On trying to get a remedy for my problem, I visited the eye specialist back in 1995.
The doctor advised me to use first, eye drops, after which he told me to use spectacles.
These are some of the spectacles I used back then.
It was purely by grace that I discovered Emmanuel TV.
I stayed glued to it after seeing the wonderful works of our Lord Jesus Christ.
Then, something miraculous happened to me!
To the glory of God, I received my healing
by simply praying along with Prophet T.B. Joshua on Emmanuel TV.
After that prayer, I could read words displayed on the television
and I was able to read messages on my phone without using spectacles.
I am totally healed by Jesus. I can read and write without using spectacles.
I am going to read you Psalm 117.
"Praise the Lord, all you nations; extol Him, all you peoples.
For great is His love towards us, and the faithfulness of the Lord endures forever.
Praise the Lord."
Thank You, Jesus; I'm totally healed!
I can read and write without spectacles.
Thank You, Jesus!
