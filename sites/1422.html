Emmanuel!
(God With Us – Matthew 1:23)
My name is Mziwoxolo Nkqayi, I come from South Africa.
I work as a police officer in SASP (South African Police Service).
Good morning, church.
My testimony goes like this:
I grew up as a person who did not know God.
I would go to church only when I saw my wife and children going to church.
Going to the church was not what I used to do because I did not know God.
I would go to church once in five or seven years.
To me, since I did not know God, I felt like something must be happening in church.
I thought that they only preached about giving ten percent.
I felt like whenever they saw me in the church, they would only preach about giving ten percent.
How can you preach to a person who does not know God, saying, “Throw money”?
I hope we understand what our brother is saying.
He grew up from a background he did not know God,
he was very rarely attending church,
and he realised that anytime he went to church the sermons that were usually preached
revolved around giving money and this discouraged him from even attending church.
Go ahead, sir.
As I became busy, I wanted to know God.
I didn’t know what it was but it kept telling me that I must seek the face of God,
I wanted to go to church.
There was one particular night I slept and in a dream, I saw a man I have never seen.
He had a dark skin colour and was not too tall but was a bit taller than me and had a lot of hair.
This dream I had was vivid in my mind.
Even during the day, it was too vivid so I started to think that I must do something about it.
Then, I remember when I was growing up, there were the elders who used to ask us
about our dreams and associate them with ancestors.
I started to think that I might need to go for a ritual and see the elders
to tell them about the man I saw when I was asleep.
Something happened.
I was sitting at home, scrolling channels to look for one where I could listen to the Word of God.
I saw this man that I saw when I was asleep, the man I had never seen anywhere.
As I was watching, I discovered that it was Emmanuel TV.
I wanted to know where the church was because of people sharing very interesting testimonies.
I told myself, “I want to go to The SCOAN. Where is the church located?”
As I was watching, I realised it was the prophet who I had dreamt of.
Then I became so happy because I was no longer thinking about going to slaughter a cow.
The person I saw in the dream – I could see he was a pastor.
The first day, I was listening to his preaching and he was telling the congregation,
“Believe and see” – that was the scripture that he was telling the congregation about.
I was very interested in what the prophet was going to say and he said, “Believe and see”.
I kept on watching Emmanuel TV and on the screen it was saying, “Touch the screen”.
I would touch the screen and pray with the Prophet.
I kept telling my family that I would be promoted next year.
My family could not understand and asked what I meant by ‘next year’, when I applied already.
I said it was because I would go to Nigeria to get the Morning Water
so that I could minister it to myself and then I would be promoted.
My family didn’t understand what I was talking about but I kept touching the screen and praying
as if I was in The SCOAN.
“Out! Amen!” – it was like I was in The SCOAN.
Whenever Prophet T.B. Joshua prayed, I would pray along with him.
For the post that I applied to, applicants were so many – more than 80
and one person was needed to fill that post.
People who had applied for this particular post had many qualifications
which we cannot even mention all of them but I applied.
I was waiting and I received a phone call that I must come because I was promoted.
Let's put our hands together for Jesus Christ!
By the grace of God, I was promoted to the next rank, ‘Captain’.
I was in a warrant officer rank and in my country, it is those who are marching in one place.
Many people retired in that rank; it is not easy to be promoted to another rank.
But now, by the grace of God, through praying along with Prophet T.B. Joshua,
you are promoted to the rank of a captain in a South African Police Force.
By the grace of God, I am a captain in SAPS.
As I grew up, I noticed that I had diabetes.
I would go to the doctors and the result would show that I had diabetes.
I was always on diabetic treatment.
I came to The SCOAN because I always wanted to step my foot in The SCOAN.
I told myself, “I will go to The SCOAN. I don’t know how much it will cost
but I will be in Lagos, Nigeria. I want to be in The SCOAN.”
In April, I came to The SCOAN on my own and was opportuned to get the Morning Water
and receive a touch from the man of God.
My results are here.
On 11th of May, I went to my doctor.
I don't know what for I went to that doctor.
I realised that I no longer have diabetes and it was only a month after I came back from Nigeria.
If you look at the result, it is written that when I went for the test on 08/01/2016,
the rate of my sugar level was 15.1 mmol/u.
After I came to The SCOAN, my sugar level went down to 6.6 mmol/u
Let's put our hands together for Jesus Christ!
The test was on 11th of May.
Just before I came to The SCOAN in the beginning of September,
the doctor told me that I didn’t have diabetes anymore and I was healed.
Let's put our hands together for Jesus Christ!
Not only was our brother promoted, he was also healed from diabetes
and we give all the glory to God Almighty.
When I had diabetes, I used to go to the toilet frequently.
During the night, I would go to the toilet seven to eight times and my wife wouldn’t notice.
I no longer have diabetes.
Amen! Let's put our hands together for Jesus Christ!
I was not a Christian but now I am a Christian.
I can feel that I am a Christian and I became a Christian through Emmanuel TV.
I would also advise people that we need to follow Jesus Christ like children following their parents.
We need to seek the living church; we should not stay in a church because we are in high positions.
We need to follow Jesus Christ like children following their parents.
TT Robert Begier 15/05/2019
