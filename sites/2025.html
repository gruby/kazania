My name is Boniface Nwaokolo.


The problem that brought me to The SCOAN was tongue cancer.


This was a problem I had never seen in my life.


It disturbed me to the extent that I couldn't eat, drink or have peace of mind.


I couldn't sleep.


This problem had taken me to so many places - chemists and hospitals.


The last hospital I attended said I would have to be operated on.


I thought to myself, "I have never seen such a thing - the tongue being operated on."


All my hope was gone.


Whenever I went home, I would encourage my family.


Even the doctor told me to hurry, saying that the problem could kill.


By God's grace, I met someone and told her about the problem I had.


She advised me that the problem would be solved if I came to The SCOAN.


I said, "Synagogue?" She said, "Yes."


On that day, I woke up very early.


I didn't know about this place before.


I came here with faith.


When I got to the gate, I was taken to the emergency section.


When Prophet T.B. Joshua came out, he looked at my tongue.


He told me to stand up, which I did.


He stretched forth his hand and prayed for me.


Immediately, the severe headache I was having went away.


All the symptoms I was experiencing disappeared.


Afterwards, he told me to go, that I was free.


Going home that day, I went back with joy because I saw changes in my body.


I didn't pay any money; they even gave me money.


How is the condition right now?


I am very fine!


Let us see the tongue; seeing is believing.
