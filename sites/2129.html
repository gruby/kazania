God can use any medium to express Himself.
An Emmanuel TV Partner in Zimbabwe
had sent an email to a colleague
with just a picture of the New Anointing Sticker
he had freely received.
CCTV in her workplace captured the footage
as she prayed with the New Anointing Sticker
alongside another colleague.
Watch what happens moments later.
This is the moment that Precious, a Zimbabwean, at work
prays with the Anointing Sticker through the screen.
By faith, she touches the Anointing Sticker
and then invites her friend to do the same.
NOT MINE BUT GOD'S OWN IN JESUS' NAME
All of a sudden, within moments of touching the screen displaying the New Anointing Sticker,
something wonderful happens
as Precious has an unforgettable encounter -
the anointing that breaks every yoke!
After this powerful experience
she shares her wonderful testimony.
Emmanuel!
(God With Us - Matthew 1:23)
My name is Precious. I come from Zimbabwe.
I would like to testify to the goodness of the Lord, through the New Anointing Sticker.
In the last two weeks of December 2020, I had a fever which caused me pain in my legs.
I also had headaches and body weakness.
I couldn't even go to work from 29th to 31st of December because of the fever.
By the grace of God, I managed to receive the New Anointing Sticker through my phone
from an Emmanuel TV Partner, who sent it to me.
So, I decided to pray with the Anointing Sticker.
I connected my phone to the computer
and I prayed together with my colleague at work with the Anointing Sticker.
I felt a sensation in my body.
After a few minutes, I felt another sensation,
but it was so much that I couldn't control myself.
I manifested and fell from my chair.
I managed to see what happened because the incident was recorded on the CCTV.
I would like to thank God
because after I prayed with the New Anointing Sticker, the following day I woke up feeling fine.
Everything had disappeared!
The body weakness had disappeared!
The headaches had stopped.
I just want to thank God for this New Anointing Sticker, which Prophet T.B. Joshua has sent to us.
Thank you!
Indeed, distance is not a barrier to God's power.
