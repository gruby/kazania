My name is Mrs Comfort Akpovoka, I am from Delta State.
On Sunday, I was sitting down in the congregation.
As the prayer was going on, I felt a sensation from my head to my feet.
I began to shake uncontrollably and then found myself at the front of the church.
When you found yourself at the front of the church, can you tell us what happened next?
I don't remember anything except that I was shaking.
When I came to my senses, the people I came with were telling me that I was
saying, "My bag, my bag!" "I want to go!" "Give me my bag!"
I was surprised and did not know that I was saying all those things.
It was the evil spirit that was in me that was manifesting
and I thank God that I am delivered, now.
Tell us the problem that brought you to The SCOAN.
I was having a spiritual husband and was seeing snakes in my dreams.
