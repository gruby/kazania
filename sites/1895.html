My name is Mercy Attaidu.
I am from Kogi State, Nigeria.
The problem that brought me to The SCOAN was difficulty in walking.
On 15th March I was called for an interview

On getting there, I met a large crowd.
I tried to go back, but my mind told me that I should just wait.
As more and more people came, I decided to go back,
but there was no way I could turn back anymore.

and they took us to hospital.
I was unconscious and on oxygen for one week.
It was a week later that I knew I was in the hospital.
