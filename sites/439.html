This man makes his way to The Synagogue Church Of All Nations hopping on one leg.
He makes use of a walking stick which he uses to aid his mobility.
He sits down after a hard struggle.
Why is he here?
A foot ulcer due to a gun shot.
This is the problem that has brought him to The Synagogue Church Of All Nations.
The top of his leg gives an idea of the condition his skin is in.
The camera pans down his leg
and on his foot it reveals unbroken skin and the ghastly open wound.
This is the foot ulcer that has hindered his ability to walk,
and is the cause of the pain and discomfort he has been enduring on a daily basis.
It oozes blood and pus, and has broken down layers of the skin.
We can see the discolouration of the surrounding skin, which has become darkened and black.
The condition of the foot ulcer is so terrible that he is unable to bear weight on his foot.
Hence the reason for his hopping in with the walking stick.
As a result of the ulcer, the foot has become swollen, rendering him unable to wear shoes.
Satan is the author of sickness and affliction.
His diseases are thieves of time, money, effort and joy.
Imagine a young man in this condition unable to work or fend for himself.
His problem has defied all medical solution,
and The Synagogue Church Of All Nations is his final hope, his last bus stop.
He has come with the belief that no one is hopeless whose hope is in the Lord.
He is in need of a radical transformation that only Jesus can give.
He sits pitifully holding up his placard.
