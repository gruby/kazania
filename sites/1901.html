1901


My name is John Okoh, I'm a native of Cross River State.
I had a case with my management in 1989.
I was a storekeeper with a construction firm affiliated with the NDA (Nigerian Defence Academy)
in Kaduna State, Nigeria.
I was with the company for over six years.
I was given sick leave for two weeks on 2nd September 1989
to travel home so that I would take care of myself.
On the 17th before coming back, I was arrested and taken to the police station,
State CID Kaduna, for the reason that I collaborated with twenty armed robbers
to rob my department.
I was later tried by the military tribunal
and sentenced to death by hanging.
After a one year interval, I was commuted to life imprisonment.
All this while, I was in Kaduna State prison.
Until last year October, I was transferred to Calabar Prison.
In Calabar Prison, there is what we call 'Emmanuel Day', that is the 'T.B. Joshua Day'.
We would watch Emmanuel TV.
We were watching the television at that time and the man of God prophesied.
That was the first time I came across the man of God, Prophet T.B. Joshua.
I had never come across him before over the 22 years I was in prison.
I had lost my freedom.
That day, I heard the man of God prophesy that some people were in prison
and someone there was about to be set free.
I jumped up and said I was the one; I claimed it.
After two weeks, the Governor of Cross River State sent a note
that all the names of the inmates should be compiled and sent to his office for freedom.
All of us that were watching that programme were ten in number.
All of us were serving life imprisonment.
When the Governor saw the list, all the ten of us who were imprisoned for life
that were watching Emmanuel TV, including myself, were released.
The SCOAN Sunday Service
As I went back home, I told myself that if I didn't come back to testify to
the goodness of what the Lord has used Prophet T.B. Joshua to prophesy concerning my life, it would be bad.
Many have tried to get me out of that position, but it was impossible.
Until this day, after 22 years in prison, I find myself a free man.
I stand before you as brethren to tell you that it is good for everybody,
even those in the prison, to watch Emmanuel TV.
By the grace of God, through the man of God and Emmanuel TV
many people are receiving their healing in prison.
And so sir, may God who has given you this great commission continue to lead you.
Sir, this is a photo of my prison cell.
Emmanuel TV and Partners all over the world, we give 200 thousand Naira.
Thank you, man of God. Thank you, sir.
Give him five bags of rice.
We don't want you to jump into society the way you find society.
Prophet T.B. Joshua along with the support of our Emmanuel TV Partners
gave a cash gift of 200 thousand Naira and five bags of rice to Mr. John Okoh,
to start his life afresh in Christ Jesus.
But by the grace of God, God has channelled the man of God,
to pray and prophesy for my freedom.
Today I am a free man.
What the man of God has given me today is enough to start
my new beginning.
It is a great privilege for me to say that I am starting a brand new life.
God has blessed me with the sum of 200 thousand Naira and five bags of rice.
Thank You, Jesus!
