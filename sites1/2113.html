I have received thousands of emails on the pandemic vaccine.
No doubt, there are many conspiracy theories surrounding this vaccine.
However, my belief is that
our governments are only trying to look for a way out of this very challenging situation,
although there may be bad faith in certain quarters.

At this time, our governments need our support
and our cooperation because the world is in a dilemma.
