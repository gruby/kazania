My name is Makanjuola Ademoyegun. I am 26 years old.
The problem that brought me here is fire burns.
It all started one night. I and my fiancee were inside.
We have been having some little problems, although there is love between us.
We actually love each other.
One night I discovered that having misunderstanding every time
without understanding each other, we should decide to quit.
I just wanted to see how much she loved me and how much I loved her.
So she decided that she is not quitting the relationship with me.
After the argument, I don't know how she came about petrol.
She brought in petrol, poured some on me and some on herself.
Instead of the two of us to quit the relationship, we would rather die together.
She set herself ablaze.
In the process of rescuing her, I was in contact with petrol too.
So, I was actually burnt as well.
It has been very terrible.
I have been having severe pain, sleepless nights, all over my body.
I was taken to LUTH - Lagos State University Teaching Hospital,
where I have been treated.
Since then the problem still persists because of the pain.
I believe today that God is going to heal me.

My name is Makanjuola Ademoyegun.

Actually we had been having some misunderstanding in our relationship,

I was lying down in the sitting room.
She went out and poured fuel into a paint bucket.
She brought it inside the sitting room, poured some on me and some on herself.
Before I could stop her, she lit the fire.
We were both set ablaze.
So, the neighbours came in when they heard noise.
They rescued the two of us.
They extinguished the fire and we were rushed to the general hospital.
Some few days later, she gave up.
After two or three weeks, my aunty came to the hospital and saw my condition
and she said it is better to take me to The Synagogue for the pastor to pray for me
rather than wasting money at the general hospital.
So, I was brought down to The SCOAN and the pastor prayed for me that day.
There was a lot of pain all over my body.
In fact, I cannot really define the type of pain I was going through that time.
I could not even stand upright but when the pastor prayed for me,
I felt relief from the inside of me and the pain was gone.
I started receiving strength and the wounds were healing.
Here I am today, giving thanks to God Almighty.
