Our names are Mr Pascal Mpoy
and the person beside me is Mrs Gillian Mpoy
and the little one is Miss Precious Mpoy.
We come from South Africa.
You are very welcome. Please tell us your testimony.
Our testimony goes like this:
At the time the child was born,
later on, after the check-up,
they discovered that she had a hole in her heart.
I was pressurised by the doctor to do surgery to close the pipe in her heart
and the documentation was sent to me to approve and go ahead with the surgery.
I was informed about the surgery
but I delayed it because
according to that procedure,
they had to use a PDA
which is a device which had to be implanted in her heart.
I questioned the doctor to find out the advantages and disadvantages of this surgery.
I was told that the only advantage was
that the procedure would trigger a spring
which would push the device to close the pipe in the heart.
The disadvantage was that
the spring would trigger the device and remain in her heart.
Then I couldn't take any decision at that time.
As a couple, my wife and I had to decide whether we should go ahead with the surgery or not.
I thank God that the doctor was a believer as well and was aware of The SCOAN.
I told him that we first wanted to find out God's opinion about the surgery.
The doctor told us that based on his medical knowledge,
he would not recommend releasing the child in that condition.
However, he would agree to do so.
But he asked whether we were able to bring the child to The SCOAN.
At that time, the Morning Water was ministered to the child.
We went back again for another check-up.
They examined the child and discovered that the hole was completely healed.
Hallelujah! Let's put our hands together for Jesus.

and the doctor examined the child,
he re-examined her three times.
He said, "This is a miracle! The noise I used to hear previously has disappeared completely".
We were very amazed and it brought peace to us.
We were so happy and we have been thanking God since then.
Hallelujah! Let's put our hands together for the King of glory.

Morning Water Testimony
My name is Gillian Mpoy. We are from South Africa.
This situation was very terrible.
I remember they gave us a machine which we had to put next to the child every time she slept
so that if something went wrong,
the alarm would be triggered
and we would wake up to see whether the child was still alive.
In that situation it is impossible to sleep properly or even focus on what you are doing.
I said to my husband, "No, we are believers. We must not do the surgery.
Her heart is in God's hands.
We can't control her heart. Let's take this machine back to the hospital".
We took it back and stayed at home with the child, watching Emmanuel TV and praying.
And we were fine.
My advice is that life is full of challenges.
Don't think that if you are a Christian you won't go through something like this.
If challenges come, just stand strong through the Word of God, in Jesus' name.
Amen. Let's put our hands together for Jesus.
