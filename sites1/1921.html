Prophet T.B. Joshua says: The prayers offered on Emmanuel TV are not time-bound.
Anytime you hear them, the presence of God is maintained.
Be blessed as you listen to this testimony
from a Togolese international footballer who discovered this reality in his own life.
There is never a sickness Jesus cannot cure, even the deadly virus COVID-19.
My name is Dove Abotchi; I am a Togolese international, I play for the Togo National Team.
I'm in South Africa playing for a football team here but I used to represent Togo in international competitions.
Unfortunately, I was tested positive for COVID-19.
It was a difficult situation for me and also for my teammates.
We were three who tested positive for COVID-19
so they quarantined us and we had to stay isolated for 14 days.
In my self-isolation time, it was tough;
I was coughing, sneezing and had a high fever.
I could not breathe properly.
It was very tough.
During our 14 days of quarantine, as I was following Emmanuel TV,
I saw that people used to call from all over the world who had been tested positive
and the ministers of God prayed for them and they were healed.
I know that the man of God, Prophet T.B. Joshua used to say that distance is not a barrier;
God is a Spirit.
On a Saturday morning, T.B. Joshua was preaching on Emmanuel TV.
After the preaching, he started praying for the viewers.
Viewers all over the world,
we say every shame the evil spirit has caused you - be it in your health -
be healed, in the name of Jesus!
Every shame the evil spirit has caused you in your health, blood, fluids -
we remove that affliction, in the name of Jesus!
That affliction - leave, in the name of Jesus!
I told myself, "This is the right time for me to get healed."
So I knelt down as he was praying for viewers and touched the screen.
During the prayer, it felt like I was no longer there and I experienced heat in my body.
I was sweating.
After the prayer, I tried to do what I was not able to do before -
like breathing, talking quickly, doing something strenuous.
So, I breathed and realised there was nothing wrong - I was normal.
I went out and walked quickly and did something fast
so that I could observe my breathing and nothing was wrong - it was normal!
Then I shouted!
I went back home and started thanking God and singing.
It was on Saturday I prayed with T.B. Joshua - that time it was not live.
Viewers, those on the sickbed, I release you from that bed of sickness!
Be released from that bed of sickness!
Be released from that bed of sickness, affliction, disease!
Be released!
T.B. Joshua said, "This prayer is not time-bound;
whenever you see this prayer and receive it, you will be healed."
I had to go back on Monday to redo the test.
I stood on this word and I received the result of the second test and it was negative!
I have to thank God for His glory and for everything He has done for me.
I'm completely healed! I started running and doing my professional training.
I started doing everything that I was doing before.
So, I have to thank God for everything He has done for me.
For those people who are in the same situation, it is not the end of the world; don't panic!
God uses everything for His name to be glorified!
So, as a Christian, don't panic.
Just have faith and keep praying and following T.B. Joshua's ministry because it is powerful!
I thank God for everything!
Thank God. Today, I'm free! I can do whatever I want!
I'm doing professional training! I'm free!
I thank God for everything He has done for me!
