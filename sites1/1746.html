1746


Viewers all over the world, be in line with us - be in one accord with us.
All the doors opened to the enemy
through which fear,
oppression is entering into your life -
by the key of David,
right now begin to shut it up, lock it up!
Lock it up!
I lock you up, in the name of Jesus!
Viewers all over the world, begin to lock it up!
Fear of affliction, stagnation,
disappointment, setback -
viewers all over the world - lock it up!
By the key of David, lock it up!
Lock it up, in the name of Jesus!
In Jesus Christ's name we pray!
The fear of stagnation, failure, setback -
whatever you might have been fearing - by the key of David, be locked up, in the name of Jesus!
All powers that have been holding you down
to where you do not belong -
there are many places you are that you do not belong.
Say, "I know where I belong".
Sometimes you find yourself in a position and you say, "I know where I belong.
I do not belong here. I know where I belong".
But you have no choice.
It is the will of God for us to prosper,
have good health, be the head and not the tail.
All powers that have been holding you down
to where you do not belong -
right now, begin to break it -
power of stagnation, failure, career failure,
affliction, sickness and disease.
Open your lips!
Pray Along With Prophet T.B. Joshua
Touch Your Screen By Faith In Jesus' Name
TT Pastor Daniel Yeboah 30-3-20
RV: Mel Flowers 31/03/2020
