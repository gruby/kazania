My name is Augustine Joseph.

I woke up one morning around 10 am, dressed up, prepared to go to work.

I called my wife and asked her to get some pills for me from the pharmacy.
She went to the pharmacy, got me the pills which I took but the pain persisted.
After a month without any solution in sight, I decided to go to the hospital.
After running all the tests, it was discovered that I had lumbar spondylosis.
I asked the doctor the way forward and he said I had to undergo an operation.
The doctor said it would be a 50-50 chance -
it would either be successful or I would end up in a wheelchair.
I objected to the operation and walked out.
The pain was so serious as it deprived me of many things, physically and psychologically.
