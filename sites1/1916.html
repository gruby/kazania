MORRIS CERULLO
1931 - 2020
On Saturday 11th of July 2020
God's General Dr Morris Cerullo was called home to eternal glory as a citizen of Heaven.
As Philippians 3:20-21 says,
"But our citizenship is in heaven. And we eagerly await a Saviour from there,
the Lord Jesus Christ, who, by the power that enables him to bring everything under his control,
will transform our lowly bodies so that they will be like his glorious body."
The legacy of Dr Morris Cerullo will forever live on in the hearts of believers worldwide,
especially the millions who came to the knowledge of saving grace through his ministry.
Raised in an orthodox Jewish orphanage, Morris had received a divine call to preach,
when he was only 15 years old.
