BREAKING NEWS
Statement From Prophet T.B. Joshua On Reopening Of Churches By The Nigerian Government
Viewers, don't forget there was a revelation three times concerning the church closing.
Let us watch the prophecy.
On Sunday, 24th February 2019, Propphet T.B. Joshua gave a prophetic message
about a vision God had given him concerning the SCOAN service.
These were his words.
The thing God is still telling me -
you will just get here one day,
you will learn that there is no church.
Please, meet me on Emmanuel TV - no church.
So, you'll meet me on Emmanuel TV talking to you,
or when there is a revival, you will see me.
So, we have to be serious.
On Sunday, 7th April 2019, Prophet T.B. Joshua prophesied further
about the vision he had seen regarding the church service at The SCOAN.
This is what he said.
You know, I told you people of recent:
You may be here, you'll learn that there is no service - close the church.
You meet us on Emmanuel TV.
Now, we are in a perilous time - a very difficult time.
Hear what is happening all over the world.
Fearful year!
A lot happened and our protection is God.
On Sunday, 5th January 2020, for the third time, Prophet T.B. Joshua once again prophesied
about what he saw would happen this year concerning The SCOAN service.
This is what he said.
I'm so concerned about what is going to happen this year -
so much concerned than anything.
If it's going to cause me to close the church and find out what is going to happen -
because everywhere is fear.
If something wants to catch you, it should not catch you unprepared.
You don't mind but I am very, very concerned.
Viewers, that same God we are waiting to hear from on when to reopen –
when things will go back to normal – as usual.
God’s time is the best.
We are waiting at His feet for that time.
We are tarrying for the Holy Spirit’s instruction.
Pray without ceasing (1 Thessalonians 5:17).
Meet us as usual on Emmanuel TV until we hear from God when to reopen,
the same God who gave us the prophecy three times about the church closing.
It is God’s work, not our work.
Here, our thanks go to the authorities for understanding.
Christian seek not yet repose
Hear thy guardian angel say
Thou art in the midst of foes
Watch and pray
Jesus is Lord!
TT Gary Tonge 03/06/2020
