Thank You, Jesus!
Let someone say, "Thank You, Jesus!"
Thank You, Jesus, from the bottom of my heart!
Lift your hands and give thanks to Him.
Thank You, Jesus!
You may be seated.
You are welcome in His name.
Say, "Neighbour, you are welcome in His presence".
Jesus Christ is the reason we are here today - our Healer, our Deliverer and our Lord.
His name be glorified, in Jesus' name.
Ask your neighbour, "How are you today?"
In Jesus' name.
Thank You, Lord.
Jesus is the reason for our gathering today.
He is the same yesterday, today and forever.
The Bible says that there is power in the name of Jesus Christ.
There is power in the name of Jesus Christ.
But if you do not use that name, Jesus Christ, in love and in faith, that name can do nothing.
If you use that name, Jesus Christ, in faith and in love,
that name will be as if it was on the lips of Apostle Peter in the Book of Acts 3:4-6.
Peter said to the crippled man, "Silver and gold I do not have, but what I have I give you.
In the name of Jesus Christ, rise and walk",
and the Bible says the lame began to walk.
That is the meaning of the name, Jesus Christ, on the lips of Apostle Peter.
But when you call that name, Jesus, without faith, nothing will happen.
When you call that name, you must call that name in Spirit and in faith.
If not, there will be no more evidence on the outside.
There are many good churches today who have a pastor who is a good teacher of the Bible
and the people understand the Scriptures.
But the real power of God is not operating in their midst.
When you ask the seven sons of Sceva in the Book of Acts 19,
they will say that if you call that name, Jesus, if that name is not planted in your heart,
that name will be meaningless and oftentimes destructive.
Tell your neighbour, "Praying is not saying words but praying the prayer".
Praying is not saying words but praying the prayer.
This brings us to our message this morning: The Prayer Of Faith.
Tell your neighbour, "The Prayer Of Faith".
Praying is not saying words but praying the prayer.
Let me take you to the Book of 2 Corinthians 4.
I will start my reading from verse 13.
"And since we have the same spirit of faith, according to what is written,
'I believed and therefore I spoke,' we also believe and therefore speak."
Say, "I believe and therefore I speak".
That spirit of faith is expressed by believing with the heart and speaking with the mouth.
The disciples of our Lord and Saviour Jesus Christ had been delivering people from evil spirits,
casting out demons but the Bible says in the Book of Mark 9,
that certain day, they met a stiff opposition.
There was a day, they had an encounter with a demon-possessed boy
and they were unable to deliver the boy.
And the father of the demon-possessed boy looked at them with a bad impression;
a lot of things were going through his mind.
Then he began to look for Jesus.
When Jesus came, the Bible says, Jesus rebuked the evil spirit
and the boy was delivered at once.
The disciples had the power to deliver but lacked the necessary faith to release that power.
Let someone say, "Prayer is not saying words".
Without faith, prayer is ineffective.
The man of God says that faith comes from the Word of God.
The Word of God is Spirit and life and has the ability to produce a spiritual force
which grows and develops within our hearts called faith.
It is acting on the Word of God that builds faith in our hearts,
not just studying the Word.
We study the Word and act on the Word
until it gets into our blood and gains supremacy over us.
For the Word to get into our blood, it means the Word becomes an integral part of us.
For the Word to gain supremacy, it means that faith comes
when the Word prevails over our thinking processes, not our way, not our mind.
Let someone say again, "Prayer is not saying words".
When we say, "Prayer is not saying words,"
God does not need my words to move Him into action
because God has His own way of implementing His will in the universe.
Therefore, it becomes necessary for us to consult God so that we may know His will,
so that our actions may line up with the will of God.
How do we know the will of God?
To know the will of God, we must know the Word of God.
Man has the ability to pray, to teach, but the Holy Spirit prays as He wills,
teaches as He wills, preaches as He wills.
This means, the Word and the Spirit must come together.
Let someone say, "The Word and the Spirit must come together".
Not only must we have God's Word in order to know His will, we also need God's Spirit.
Prophet T.B. Joshua says that there is an inseparable union between the Word and the Spirit
which indicates that the Spirit always leads in prayer.
This means, it is God's power working through His Word and by His Spirit that brings about the result,
that makes that prayer effective.
Prayer is not saying words but praying the prayer.
What do we mean by 'praying the prayer'?
One of the principles that governs prayer is that of co-operating with God
in prayer through the Holy Spirit.
The Holy Spirit prays within us.
He supplies the inspiration of what to pray for.
When the word to pray comes from within,
when you speak what comes from your heart, you will have the result.
Say, "Neighbour, prayer is not saying words but praying the prayer".
The Holy Spirit prays within us.
Therefore, brethren, no matter the situation you are facing today,
no matter the trouble that brought you here today,
don't allow that situation to dictate the direction of your prayers.
Are you facing barrenness?
Are you facing difficulty in your job?
Whatever that trouble is, don't allow that situation to dictate the direction of your prayers
because God is still saying something.
Say, "Neighbour, God is still saying something".
Faith focuses on God.
No matter the trouble you have today,
the Bible says, 'Don't measure your Christian life by the challenges'.
That is why after we have prayed,
that prayer must be followed by an attitude of absolute trust in God
that He is working out the answer - stop doubting.
Say, "Neighbour, whatever you are facing now,
whatever is your challenge -
right now begin to reset your mind, begin to reset your heart
by focusing on God.
Faith focuses on God Almighty, not on the problem.
When you focus on God, you will be calm, determined and calculated
even when everything around you is unquiet.
God never promised to keep us away from tests, trials and temptation
but He promised to see us through.
Therefore, no matter what you are facing right now in your life, in your career,
don't allow this to dictate your direction and see God in a bad light.
God is still saying something. Focus on God.
If we commit our works, if we commit our ways to God,
God has promised to carry us in the arms of His strength, just as a nurse carries a child.
God has promised to strengthen our spirit with His Spirit.
Say, "Neighbour, this is the time to exchange your strength with God's strength".
Say, "Neighbour, the Word of God is a living and growing force".
Say, "Today is my day".
Do you believe that?
Say, "I am in the arena of liberty".
The prayers you have been offering are not lost.
Whatever you have been facing before was just to strengthen your belief in Christ.
That prayer is not lost.
Hallelujah.
Therefore, we should remember that scriptural prayer releases the power of God.
Tell your neighbour, "Scriptural prayer releases the power of God".
Scriptural prayer is prayer filled with the Scriptures.
It brings an answer from God because it is pregnant of faith
and faith activates prayer.
Faith energises and activates our prayer.
Faith is the force that moves our words to work.
So if the motivation to pray comes from within -
it is the indwelling Word coming from our heart, out of our lips
that produces an overcoming spirit within us.
Say, "Neighbour, it is the Word of God, the Word of heart, coming out of our mouth
that produces an overcoming spirit within us.
The extent to which you think the thoughts of Christ,
to that same extent we have the power of God.
Say, "Neighbour, the extent to which you think the thoughts of Christ,
to that extent you have the power of God.
The Bible says, "Out of the abundance of our heart, the mouth speaks".
Say, "Out of the abundance of my heart, my mouth speaks".
Say, "Prayer is not saying words but praying the prayer".
Finally brethren, we need to know:
The prayer of faith - God's Word must be the foundation.
God will not move us to pray for something that is not sanctioned by the Scriptures.
Say, "God will not move me to pray for anything that is not sanctioned by the Scriptures".
This means, to pray in the Spirit is to pray in harmony with the Word of God
and when you pray in harmony with the Word of God, you are praying with the will of God.
Therefore, fill your prayers with the Scriptures.
Using the Word of God you read daily helps ensure that your prayers line up with the will of God
and His intention for your life.
When you do that, your objectives and motives will be determined at once.
This means, you will know what God wants for you and you will know the reason God wants it.
Say, "Neighbour, this is the key - faith".
Say, "My heart is the prayer room".
Say, "My heart must be rooted, grounded in the Word of God".
When it does, faith stands
because with my heart I believe, with my mouth I confess, and faith is both.
If our hearts and our minds are filled with the Word of God by meditating on it day and night,
you will speak the Word of faith day and night.
If they attack in the dream, you speak the Word of faith.
In the day - the Word of faith; in your business - the Word of faith;
in your career - the Word of faith.
Today, the Word of faith will change your situation.
Do you believe that?
You have heard the testimonies today?
Say, "My testimony has come!"
Say, "Today!"
Can you speak to your giant?
Can you speak to your problem?
Stand up and speak to your problem!
Say, "My problem, you giant, I will not allow you to dictate the direction of my prayers.
You mountain, I command you, in the name of Jesus - give way!
Today is my day!"
Thank You, Jesus!
God bless His Word.
