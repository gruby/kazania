During this critical time, Emmanuel TV Partners all over the world
have risen to the challenge to plant love and care in the lives of those who need it most.
The nation of Israel was not left out.
In response to the need to purchase medical equipment to help treat those affected by the sickness.
in the Galilee Medical Center, the largest hospital in the Galilee region of northern Israel,
TB Joshua and Emmanuel TV presented a cheque of 100,000 shekels (30,000 dollars)
to the CEO of the Galilee Medical Center, Dr Masad Barhoum.
My name is Dr Barhoum; I am the CEO of the Galilee Medical Center.
It is the largest hospital in the Galilee region, with 723 beds.
I would like to thank you and appreciate your donation to the hospital.
It will help the staff and the patients to cope with the disease.
Thank you again and thanks especially to Emmanuel TV.
I would like to one day see you here in Israel.
Overwhelmed at the donation, the hospital published an article in appreciation on their official Facebook page.
Galilee Medical Center wrote:
Good news in difficult times - thank you to the Emmanuel Global Network for the generous donation.
Galilee Medical Center is grateful to receive some substantive and meaningful contributions
to strengthen us and to help us fight the pandemic.
The Emmanuel Global Network, a Christian organization with five branches worldwide,
generously contributed 100,000 Israeli shekels to support us in our efforts
to provide medical care to the residents of northern Israel.
Three members of the association (who asked to remain anonymous out of modesty)
met with Dr Masad Barhoum, Director of the Medical Center, and gave him a cheque
towards the purchase of life-saving equipment.
They said, "Galilee Medical Center serves a diverse population of different religions and sectors -

The hospital also sent an official letter of appreciation to T.B. Joshua and the Emmanuel TV team.
To Mr T.B. Joshua, Emmanuel TV:
Dear Friends, I would like to express my heartfelt appreciation and thank you
for reaching out to the Galilee Medical Center during this time of crisis.
The sickness outbreak is a new worldwide phenomenon, affecting all of us -
in Israel and worldwide, causing disruption and uncertainty for our families and our communities.
Galilee Medical Center is located in the City of Nahariya in the Western Galilee.
It is the only Medical Center in the region, and we dedicatedly serve a diverse population
of 600,000 Jews, Moslems, Christians and Druze.
Our strategic location, only six miles from the hostile Lebanese border, has already required us in the past
to rapidly adjust to emergency situations, including treating 3,000 wounded Syrians
who crossed the border seeking for medical help.
During this time, as the virus continues to threaten the population,
the hospital's professional staff immediately rose to the challenge.
Two virus wards have already been set up to receive patients
and a third Ward will soon be opened.
The Emmanuel Global Network gift is highly needed and appreciated, and will be used to purchase
medical equipment for our Wards to fight the disease and strengthen our community.
Thank you again for your gift, it is highly valued.
Cordially, Dr Masad Barhoum, CEO Galilee Medical Center.
