REACHING THOSE IN ISOLATION CENTRES FOR INTERACTIVE PRAYER
The blessing of Jesus is for those who knock and ask (Matthew 7:7).
We want to encourage our communities, states, local and international bodies,
medical doctors, scientists and the people in charge to organise those who are in isolation
and let us arrange how to connect them to us on Emmanuel TV.
In whatever way you support to make this happen, we are a team;
we are not doing more than you (1 Corinthians 3:8).
In any way you need our service in transmission, we are at your service.
Together we shall pray for them.
Together we shall be set free from this COVID-19.
The same anointing that heals one is also able to heal all at the same time.
Just as the Scripture says, at sunset, the people brought to Jesus
all who had various kinds of sickness and after the mass prayer,
they were all healed (Luke 4:40).

info@emmanuel.tv
Emmanuel.
God with us.
