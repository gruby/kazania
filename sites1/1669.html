TESTIMONY CONTINUES
My name is Keamogeste Oodira.
I am from Botswana.
I am 29 years old.
I want to give glory to God for delivering me from an addiction to eating raw meat.
It all started when I was 10 years old.
We were in the kitchen when a close family member came and took a big slice of uncooked meat.
He ate it and when he ate it, he looked at me in the eyes and said, "This is how we do it."
Immediately, I got the urge to eat this raw meat.
So, I started to eat it.
I ate it and whenever my parents would send me to the shops
sometimes they would tell me to buy meat for 20 Pulas.
I would bring only meat for 5 Pulas because I had eaten it on the way.
My parents would argue and say, "Why did you bring only such small portion of meat?"
But they did not know that I would steal and eat it.
It continued since then.
It affected my life from primary school onwards.
One day, when our friend introduced us to Emmanuel TV,
I saw people eating soil and other things that were not good.
I said, "This is not normal. I have to go to The SCOAN to get my deliverance."
Whenever I ate this meat, I felt something like a snake moving in my stomach.
If I didn't eat it, I felt so angry.
It went on like this, until I decided to come to The SCOAN to seek God's intervention.
It affected my life spiritually and I started to have a spiritual husband.
I had setback in my education and limitation with disappointments.
But whenever I ate raw meat, it tasted so sweet and good.
It was so creamy - like a chocolate.
There was a time when I tried to stop by myself.
Whenever I tried to stop by myself, I would have dreams;
I would be eating this raw meat with chopsticks, with dripping blood.
Sometimes I would be eating it in the dreams like an animal.
I could not stop it by myself.
That's when I decided to seek God's intervention.
Tell us what happened when you visited The SCOAN;
how you received your deliverance.
When the man of God touched me, I felt like something was coming out of my body.
After my deliverance, when I tasted this raw meat, it tasted bitter, and it smelled really bad.
So, I give glory to God for my deliverance.
Shall we put our hands together for the Lord Jesus Christ.
For the very first time, after 19 years of eating this raw meat when it tasted and smelled good -
now it tasted bitter and it smelled really bad - horrible.
I could not eat it anymore.
That Sunday night I had a dream that I had a swollen stomach.
All of a sudden, the thing that was moving in my stomach was so aggressive.
I woke up at 4 am.
I rushed to the bathroom.
When I rushed to the bathroom, something very watery and smelly came out of me.
It was the first time it has ever happened in my life.
Since then, this feeling of a snake moving up and down stopped.
The urge that I would have to eat this raw meat - it stopped.
Deliverance Testimony
Before that, cooked meat was tasteless to me.
When I was eating it, I did not want people to notice that I did not like it. I would add a lot of pepper to give it flavour.
But now to my amazement, when I eat it, I don't even have to add pepper. It tastes delicious.
And I thank God!
