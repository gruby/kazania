I greet you saints in the name of Jesus.
My name is Busi Mngomezulu.
I am from South Africa.
The problem that brought me to The SCOAN
was the fracture of the bone of my foot.
It happened recently at home. As I was excited jumping up and down, I broke one of the bones.
I visited the doctor.
He said that I should not step on the foot.
It was so painful because the bone was broken, but it was not totally broken.
He said that if I step on it, it will break into two.
So he gave me a pair of crutches and a moon boot to support the bone
and the crutches to help me walk, so that I would not step on the foot,
because I could not walk without the crutches.
So it was in this situation that you came from South Africa to The SCOAN.
Can you just describe to us what happened in the service yesterday?
As I was sitting down the man of God walked past and saw me.
He asked me what happened
and I told him that I have got a fracture and I cannot walk,
and I am wearing a moon boot for support.
He started praying for me.
The amazing thing that happened then was just the power of God that I felt -
it is something that I have never experienced in my life.
It just felt like electricity or some forceful power that was coming inside of me
and it pulled me up, because I just saw myself standing
and I started walking towards the man of God
because I saw that he was calling me, but I did not realise.
Let's put our hands together louder for Jesus Christ.
You said sister, as the man of God, Prophet TB Joshua, prayed for you
you felt something like electricity go through your body.
Can you just describe again how that happened, how that feeling was?
The way I see it in my head, it is like bolts of electricity going through your veins,
or something coming inside of you.
It is an amazing feeling.
I can't explain it; I have never felt something like that before
and I don't think any human has ever felt something like that.
We can see here. You mean sister, at this point, that is how you just began to walk for the first time since this accident -

Yes, it was the first time I started walking freely with no pain, with no crutches, no moon boot.
I thank Jesus for the healing.
Ever since then I am free, in Jesus' name.
