1979


This is T.B. Joshua praying for international visitors
who came from far and wide in search of solution
to their various predicaments they found themselves in.
Among them is Pastor Alcir Desa who came due to array of problems;
heart palpitations, fatigue - just to name a few.
After receiving a touch that pressed in to release any chain holding Pastor Desa down, he falls,
signifying the new state of his challenges - gone, in Jesus' name.
NAZARETH MEETING
WITH TB JOSHUA
TESTIMONY CONTINUES
My name is Alcir Desa.
I am originally from Brazil.
Now, I am living in USA.
I came this wonderful evening to give my testimony.
I came from a dysfunctional family; there were many problems in my family.
Because of that, I had depression.
The depression was so severe that I wanted to take my life.
I remember there was a time when I went to a subway,
I heard a voice telling me to take my life.
Even though I was a pastor, I could still hear that voice.
When I was approaching the track, the voice was telling me to jump.
Suddenly, another voice told me not to jump, so I stepped back.
This depression continued in my life.
The voice was telling me that I was not going to have a good relationship with my family.
I had infections in my stomach.
These infections caused a lot of pain in my stomach.
With this depression, I had problems with my heart - it would beat very fast.
Sometimes, I felt weak, anxious and depressed with these infections.
I looked for divine healing and testimonies about divine healing on the Internet.
I continued to watch them.
The Holy Spirit spoke to my heart to go to Lagos, to The SCOAN.
On my own, I went to The SCOAN in Lagos.
When T.B. Joshua approached me, I could feel pain and agony in my stomach.
Something was coming out of me.
T.B. Joshua prayed for me, hit my stomach and then I fell to the floor.
When I fell down, I heard a voice say, "You are free! Stand up!"
After that prayer, something came out through my rectum.
A liquid came out of me.
Now, I am free!
That happened five years ago.
Now, my life has changed!
My marriage has changed!
My ministry has changed!
My health has changed!
My heart has changed!
Now, I don't feel any pain in my stomach.
My emotions, feelings - everything now is good.
No anxiety, no depression and no evil voice talking to me.
Now, I can hear the voice of the Holy Spirit.
Hallelujah!
Brothers and sisters, this evening, I advise you to keep your faith in Jesus.
Keep your faith in God.
God can do miracles today.
He is always available for those who seek Him in faith.
Wow.
Now, for the benefit of those who are watching you all over the world,
can you just summarize your wonderful testimony in your native language, in Portuguese?
