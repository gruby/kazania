I was very touched and I cried.
After returning to Hong Kong, my mindset was totally different and I felt happy, instantly.
I no longer have the thoughts of suicide and divorce.

Anyone next to me or around her, talking anything to her,
she would think that that person doesn't love or like her.

Sometimes she even thought about dying and committing suicide.
After we got married, during these 1.5 years, she suddenly wanted to get divorced from me
and she told me a few times that she wanted a divorce.
I asked her, "Why? I did not do anything wrong towards you or in our marriage and I love you so much".
But she said, "I don't know the reason but I don't want to be married to you now, I want a divorce".
After she came back from The SCOAN to Hong Kong,
I feel that she is totally different in her mindset
because when people around her talk something negative about her
or a negative thing in the environment or the society,
she doesn't think anything negative.
She has become a very positive thinking person.
After her deliverance, I never heard that she wants to get divorced from me.
