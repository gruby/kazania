The title of our message: Don't Concentrate On Your Situation
Tell your neighbour: Don't concentrate on your situation.
What is your situation? That is the question.
Many Christians today cry, murmur and lament every day about their situations
because their lives are centred on how they are doing,
how they are feeling and how they are treated.
It is all about Jesus, not about us.
Tell your neighbour: Don't be ruled by your situation.
Don't be ruled by your situation.
Your situation could be sickness, poverty, hardship,
rejection, isolation or barrenness.
Don't be ruled by your situation.
The rich must not concentrate on their wealth.
Tell your neighbour: The rich must not concentrate on their wealth.
The poor should not concentrate on their poverty.
The sick should not concentrate on their sickness.
Tell your neighbour once again: Don't be ruled by your situation.
Let's take our reading from John 16 verse 33.
"These things I have spoken to you, that in Me you may have peace.

I have overcome the world."
'Be of good cheer' means be happy. I have overcome disease and given you healing.

But why are you still worried?
That is the question you need to ask yourself: Why am I worried?
Why are you worried?
If you are with Him, you will enjoy the peace He is talking about.
Are you with Him? That is the question you need to ask yourself: Are you with Him?
He has conquered the world and given you victory. Are you one of the people?
No matter what you are passing through,
if you are with Him, you will enjoy peace.

No matter what you are passing through,

What do I mean: You must not consider it unusual,
abnormal, odd and begin to doubt.
