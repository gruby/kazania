President John Pombe Magufuli
October 29, 1959 - March 17, 2021
On March 17, 2021 President John Pombe Magufuli
- the 5th President of Tanzania -
was called home to eternal glory.
Alongside his family and nation, Tanzania,
we at Emmanuel TV and The SCOAN
celebrate the life and legacy of President Magufuli.
No doubt, heaven is our resting place
- our final abode - and we long to be there.
Therefore, the best prayer we can offer our friends, family and loved ones
is that the Lord Jesus Christ may be with their spirits
to satisfy and save them
and to receive them to Himself at last.
To the departed soul we say, "Shalom!"
TT Rae van Zyl 21/03/2021
RV Robert Begier 21/03/2021
