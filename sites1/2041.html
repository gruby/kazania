DEPEND ON GOD'S SPIRIT! | TB Joshua Sermon
It is all about our Lord, Jesus Christ, not a god we do not know.
Take note of that - not a god we do not know.
When you worship a god you do not know, surprises come.
You ask for healing; attack comes.
You ask for deliverance; demonic attack comes.
You keep asking yourself, 'I do this, I do that...'
Are you actually depending on God's Spirit to worship Him?
That is the question.
You say, 'I ask, I pray, I fast, I do this, I do that, I sleep in the church...'
The question you need to ask yourself: Are you actually depending on God's Spirit to worship Him?
Those who worship God without depending on God's Spirit to worship Him, worship a god they do not know.
When you worship a god you do not know, surprises come.
You ask for this; you receive another thing.
The pastor, evangelist, prayer warriors - you pray and attack comes in return.
You pray for a patient, "Be healed, be delivered" - take note of the outcome of those prayers.
You see those things around you and the things within you - satan is attacking.
You are praying for people but you...
"Be delivered, in Jesus' name" - that is the common language, your 'national anthem'.
"Be delivered, in Jesus' name" - you don't need a licence to say that; nobody will arrest you; you don't even need to be a Christian to say that.
You are free, you can just say it, nobody will query you, nobody will ask you, "Why are you calling this name when it doesn't belong to you?"
Just like going to a bookshop and buying a Bible - nobody will say, "Are you a Christian? Who are you?"
No, once you give them money, they will give you a Bible.
Those who worship God without depending on God's Spirit to do so - surprises come.
The pastor, evangelist, bishop, prophet, prayer warrior - you Christians - you pray and attack comes in return.
"Be healed, be delivered, in Jesus' name. Amen."
This is why you should not condemn; when you see anyone who says he is a pastor, leave him.
If he is not depending on God's Spirit to do what he is doing, those things will come back to him.
The repercussions are great; you will pity him.
You will cry for him as time goes on.
So, that is why I don't condemn anyone.
Anyone that says, "I'm a pastor, I'm a bishop, I'm this..." - no, don't worry; don't fight for God; you can't fight for God.
He says, "I'm a pastor, I'm an evangelist, I'm this, I'm that, a bishop; in Jesus' name, amen" - don't condemn, just be praying.
Because if he is not, he will not be able to depend on God's Spirit to worship.
If he is not a genuine pastor, he will worship God without depending on God's Spirit to do so.
If I am talking to you, let me see your hand.
Can you see the difference?
If this man is a true servant of God, he will depend on God's Spirit to worship God.
But if he is not a true one, he will not be able to depend on God's Spirit to worship.
There's a way to call God, and there's a way to depend on God's Spirit to call God - there are two ways now.
You call Jesus, "Jesus" - there is one - another one is to depend on God's Spirit to call that name.
And the key to that name is Spirit.
To depend upon the Spirit to call Him; that is the key.
The Bible says that God's power works through His Word and His Spirit.
God's power cannot work without His Word and Spirit.
God's power cannot work only through His Word.
'I can preach very well, that's all' - God's power cannot work through only that.
It must work through His Word and His Spirit.
You can only study His Word in school but His Spirit - that is where the key is.
'I want to be a pastor' - now, I choose to go to theology school - there is nothing bad about that; it is good.
But what you will study there is His Word; you cannot get that Spirit there.
God has His own college and you can only get His Spirit in His college.
There is God's college, and that Spirit can only be attained or achieved in His college.
So, now, God's power cannot work only through His Word.
Now there are two servants: A genuine servant of God will depend on God's Spirit to call that name.
A 'self-servant', 'self-anointed', 'self-ordained', will call that name without depending on the Spirit of God.
If you are calling the name of God without depending on God's Spirit to call the name,
you are calling a god you do not know and it will come by surprise.
You get frustrated - "I fast, I pray, I do this and that; look at me" - at the end of the day you turn to a spiritualist;
you have to go to a witchdoctor to support yourself; if not, your life is finished.
You have to begin to go to a spiritualist, a witchdoctor, and put something in your pocket to add something to what you are doing, in support.
Because you see that name cannot get you anything; you cannot hear anything.
It is because you are not depending upon the Spirit to call that name.
The holy men were carried along by the Spirit to write and speak.
The Spirit must suggest to us what to say.
A suggestion from the Spirit, because God is not a man that has this flesh or blood.
He is not a man you can just walk to and call; that is why you have to go through His Spirit.
The Bible says that God is Spirit and those who worship Him do so in Spirit and truth.
It is all about Jesus we know, not the Jesus we do not know.
Worship Him in Spirit and truth.
