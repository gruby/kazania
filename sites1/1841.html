Greetings From Barranquilla, Colombia
Emmanuel TV viewers, we are here at Barranquilla, Colombia,
the 'Golden Gate' of our nation.
We can see the development of this beautiful city since 2007 when the Mayor of Barranquilla City,
Dr Juan García gave us the key of the city and we immediately gave it to Jesus Christ.
God has done extraordinary things here.
I went to Lagos, Nigeria, and gave the key of the city to Prophet T.B. Joshua.
Since then, the city has changed because of the prayer of the prophet.
Although it was the fourth most important city in the country, this city was not developing.
We have seen the manifestation of God's power.
Barranquilla has changed in every aspect - socially and physically.
There has been great investment and refurbishment of the sports centres.
Highways and buildings were constructed.
A 12,000 capacity conference centre was built.
God has worked through the Mayors of this city after giving the key of the city to Jesus
and has done extraordinary things in Barranquilla, the 'Golden Gate' of Colombia.
We want to thank God for the life of Prophet T.B. Joshua
because since we gave the key of the city to him, this city has experienced a supernatural change.
Despite all the challenges the country has faced, Barranquilla City has remained calm.
We give thanks to God for Emmanuel TV.
Stay tuned to Emmanuel TV at home, at work, wherever you are and you will be blessed!
Emmanuel!
Good morning!
I'm here with our big friend, Dr Juan García, former Mayor of Barranquilla, Colombia.
He is the man that God used to give the key of the city of Barranquilla to God.
The key of the city was not only given for four days but for a hundred years.
When Dr Juan García took over as mayor of the city, the first thing he thought of
was to give the key of the city to Jesus Christ.
Good Morning!
Thank you for this invitation.
We extend our greetings to Prophet T.B. Joshua.
I had the blessing to personally meet Prophet T.B. Joshua in Cali, Colombia.
I extend my warm greetings to Prophet T.B. Joshua.
I still have the gift he gave me.
It wasn't an easy assignment - I was appointed as Mayor of Barranquilla in December, 2006,
when the city was facing a difficult time.
Then, we started to work and found that there had been many attempts to give the key of the city
to the church but they had not been approved.
Therefore, we decided that this decree would be one of the first decrees to be approved.
We approved it and immediately presented it to the church.
Today, we can see the impact of this, despite all the challenges the municipality was facing.
I want to tell people listening to us that only God made this possible.
This city is changing.
In 2007, the decree was signed and the key of the city was given to the church by the Mayor,
just as it was in the Bible when the kings and governors came
to the priests and prophets, seeking guidance.
Afterwards, I thought everything in our city would start changing
until I realised that God uses higher authorities and I decided to visit Nigeria.
I met Prophet T.B. Joshua and gave him the key of the city and asked him to pray for our city.
Subsequently, everything changed when Prophet T.B. Joshua started praying for our city.
We have seen great changes in our infrastructure such as bridges, highways and the seafront -
to God be the glory!
We want to encourage all the viewers of Emmanuel TV and pastors around the world
that despite the challenges, you should give the key of your city to Jesus,
by taking it to a higher authority that is being mightily used by God.
It has been a great blessing for us and I invite all pastors to look for the key of their city.
Prophet T.B. Joshua, we want to honour and bless your life and ministry for your commitment.
We know that you are always praying for us and the city of Barranquilla.
We are so grateful to the ministry of Prophet T.B. Joshua,
The SCOAN in Lagos, Nigeria and Emmanuel TV.
Prophet, I extend my greetings to you.
I had the opportunity to meet you in Cali.
I believe not everyone has the grace to be as close to you as we were
during that five minutes, where you mentioned you will visit Barranquilla.
We kept that word and we are waiting for you.
We send a warm embrace to you and to all your international team.
We wait for you with open arms so you can see the city you prayed for.
I recognise that there are different levels in ministry
and acknowledge the superior spiritual authorities that God is using.
By the grace of God, Prophet T.B. Joshua prayed and continues praying for us.
Prophet, we bless you, in the name of the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit!
We invite you to come back to Colombia, especially Barranquilla, the 'Golden Gate.'
We bless you all!
Good Morning!
Good Morning, 2020!
