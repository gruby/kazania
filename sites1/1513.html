Viewers all over the world, wherever you are,
whatever situation you are in -
never a sickness Jesus cannot cure,
never a disease Jesus cannot heal,

Be it your liver - begin to function, in the name of Jesus!
Be it your kidneys - begin to function, in the name of Jesus!
Be it your bones, blood, tendons, fluids, tissues - begin to function, in the name of Jesus!
