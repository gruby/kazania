If you trust in your friends, neighbours, family, remember one day they can die and leave you.

Do you know the reason why I have begun today's message in this tone?
Today it is so common for people to rely on those who are unreliable,
for people to depend on those who are not dependable,
for people today to put their trust in people who are not trustworthy.
And when you do this, what happens?
You set yourself up for disappointment.

The truth is that we are living in a danger zone
where the Bible says, "Everyone lies to his neighbour."

There is no other source of truth in this world,
there is no other source of reliability in this world outside of Jesus Christ.
He is still the steadfast One, still trustworthy, still reliable.
