WE ARE ONE!!! | TB Joshua Sermon
To really show that we love Him, you must have one purpose -
salvation of our soul.
The Bible says:
The work of the Holy Spirit first begins in the understanding and is carried on
to the will, affections and conversation.
This means, to be born again is to give up to God.
The title of the message:
WE ARE ONE
1 Corinthians 12:14:
"Now the body is not made up of one part but of many."
This means, the numbers - many but the body is one.
All Christians make up one body in Christ.
Jesus is the Head of the body.
The common centre of their unity.
If Jesus is the common centre of their unity,
this means they are one body but with one purpose in life.
It is like carrying different stones, yam, cassava, sand, this, that.
That is a load inside of a basket,
but this is the body that carries it.
No matter what is inside the basket - the body that carries it is this.
It is the same body that will feel the weight.
No matter what is inside the basket.
No matter what is inside this; whether biscuits, stones - who carries it?
The numbers - many but the body - one.
What is that verse 14 trying to say?
Each of us have different ministries,
different challenges,
different operations and different gifts
but we are still on one body.
If T.B. Joshua does not recognize you, that are endowed with different gifts,
I am working for Jesus, but I am not a friend of Jesus.
Many are working for Jesus.
How many are friends of Jesus?
If you do not recognise me because I am given a different ministry,
different operation, different gift,
you are working for Jesus but not a friend of Jesus.
Because the Bible says, every member, mystically, cannot have the same office.
Let someone say, "Mystically cannot have the same place."
Each member has its particular form,
use, operation and place.
God has placed us where it pleases Him.
Are you where God wants you to be?
He is not fixed in the same station, the same place
or favoured with the same gift.
Shall we say, he does not belong to Christ?
Ask your neighbour, "Are you where God wants you to be?"
If you are where God wants you to be, His grace will be sufficient for you.
If you are where God wants you to be, He will always speak strength
and courage through your trials.
Many of us are not where God wants us to be.
That is why when trouble comes, we abandon our post,
we go out of our way.
And that trouble is supposed to be a tonic to our anointing,
if you are where God wants you to be.
Because the Bible says in the Book of Mark 12:10:
"The stone which the builders rejected has become the chief cornerstone.
It is the Lord's doing, and it is marvellous in our eyes."
The Bible says man's rejection provokes God's direction.
When man rejects you, God will direct you.
When you were rejected, did you experience God's direction?
If truly you are a child of God, that persecution is the Lord's doing,
so that He can use it to bring the best out of you.
That hatred of men - people hate you - it is the Lord's doing, God allowed it to happen,
so that He can bring the best out of you.
If you are where God wants you to be, your struggle will make you stronger.
1 Corinthians 12:22:
"On the contrary, those parts of the body that seem to be weaker are indispensable,
and the parts that we think are less honourable we treat..."
What does this mean?
Imagine: if everyone loved me and accepted me,
I would grow up being a weakling.
Do you know what I mean by 'weakling'?
That is depending on the people to show me the way.
When you make God all and all, you will have direction.
Tell your neighbour, "Man's rejection, provokes God's direction."
The question: Are you where God wants you to be?
If you are not where God wants you to be, you will always go out of your way.
When you are where God wants you to be,
where God guides, where God be - protection be.
If you are not where God wants you to be,
you will always depend on what you see, what you hear and what others tell you.
Remember, we walk by faith, not by sight.
The problem most of us are facing; we have been renovated externally.
But the Bible is talking about internal renovation and drinking into one Spirit.
Not external renovation - internal renovation.
Until you are renovated on the inside, you will not be able to maintain your union with Him.
Tell your neighbour, "Until you are renovated on the inside,
you cannot maintain your union with Christ."
What do I mean by 'union with Christ'?
Union with Christ is our walking with Him.
Good times, hard times.
When you are renovated on the inside, you will see your hard times
as an opportunity for you to honour God before men.
Look at Daniel,
Meshach, Shadrach, Abednego,
Joseph, Moses.
Do you see your encounter with hard times as an opportunity for you to honour God,
or do you see it as an opportunity to run helter-skelter?
You are looking for blessing, money, you are so desperate.
When you are desperate to get blessing, you will not mind the source of your blessing.
When satan came to Jesus and asked Him,
"You are hungry. Here is the food",
Jesus said, "Look, it is true I am hungry, but I am not desperate at all."
When you know that you are a part of the body; remember, we have one purpose in life.
Satan always uses our situation - he knows your situation.
Immediately he realises that this man is sick,
he will begin to move around, using that situation to tempt you.
Many of us have gone out of our way, our duty, forfeited God's promise.
Pride is the major problem we are facing.
Pride does not allow us to acknowledge another's gifts.
Anything you do not have is not from God - that is pride.
No one can succeed alone.
The minutest member is a part of the body - tell your neighbour.
All His members, He honours dearly.
Pride is a sin in us by nature
and we must be armed against it
because all believers make up one body in Christ.
Because the Bible says, God has placed us where it pleases Him.
Are you where God placed you?
If you are not there, you will go out of your way, your duty.
Blessed is the man who follows the path of God.
Even in darkness, light shines for the upright.
Everyone has dark moments.
What is your dark moment?
Your dark moment could be poverty, isolation, rejection, name calling, false accusation,
nightmare, fear.
If truly you are a child of God, God will speak strength and courage through your darkness.
If your enemy uses iron to block your way,
God will call a welder to cut the iron.
